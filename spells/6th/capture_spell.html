Capture Spell

6th-level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 60 feet of you casting a spell
Range: 60 feet
Components: S
Duration: Instantaneous

You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell and steal its magical energies for yourself. If the creature is casting a spell of 6th level or lower, its spell fails and has no effect. If it is casting a spell of 7th level or higher, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the spell’s level. On a success, the creature’s spell fails and has no effect.

If you successfully cause a spell to fail using this spell, you can choose expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less than the level of the failed spell, and none of the slots can be 6th level or higher.

If you are using the spell points variant rule and successfully cause a spell to fail using this spell, you recover a number of spent spell points equal to the spell point cost of the failed spell.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the interrupted spell has no effect if its level is less than or equal to the level of the spell slot you used.

Spell Lists. Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

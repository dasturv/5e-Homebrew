Reflexive Bastion

4th-level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you or an creature within 10 feet of you takes damage
Range: Self (10-foot radius)
Components: S
Duration: Instantaneous

You call forth a shimmering golden aura of protective power, shielding yourself and your allies from the brunt of an attack. You and each creature of your choice within 10 feet of you reduce the damage taken by 3d8, provided they would take damage from the same source, such as a fireball spell or dragon's breath weapon. This reduction is only applied to the single instance of damage, and does not reduce any subsequent damage, even if it is from the same source.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage reduction is increased by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists. Artificer, Cleric, Paladin

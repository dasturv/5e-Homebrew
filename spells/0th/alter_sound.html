Alter Sound

Transmutation cantripSonomancy

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V
Duration: Up to 1 minute

You manipulate sound within range. You create one of the following magical effects within range:

  • You cause a source of sound is amplified to up to three times as loud as normal for 1 minute.
  • You cause a source of sound to be muffled to to a third of its normal volume for 1 minute.
  • You cause a source of sound to be distorted in a way of your choice for 1 minute, such as causing echoes, making someone sound higher pitched than normal or adding a vibrato to the sound.
  • You create an instantaneous sound that originates from a point of your choice within range, such as a rumble of thunder, a short musical motif or ominous whispers.

If you cast this spell multiple times, you can have up to three of its 1-minute effects active at a time, and you can dismiss such an effect as an action.

Spell Lists. Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard

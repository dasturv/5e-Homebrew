Blinding Flash

2nd-level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (a small mirror or polished stone)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You let out a bright flash of light, blinding those around you. Each creature within 10 feet of you that can see you must make a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, that creature is blinded until the spell ends. At the end of each of its turns, a creature can repeat this saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using certain higher-level spell slots, radius of the spell increases: up to 15 feet with a 4th-level slot, 20 feet with a 6th level spell slot and 30 feet with an 8th-level slot.

Spell Lists. Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard

Reflexive Barrier

2nd-level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you take damage
Range: Self
Components: S
Duration: Instantaneous

You call forth a shimmering golden shield of arcane power, absorbing some of the damage of an incoming attack. You reduce the damage taken by 3d8.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage reduction is increased by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists. Sorcerer, Wizard

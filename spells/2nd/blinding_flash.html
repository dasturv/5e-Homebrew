Blinding Flash

2nd-level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (a small mirror or polished stone)
Duration: Instantaneous

You let out a bright flash of light, blinding those around you. Each creature within 10 feet of you that can see you must make a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, that creature is blinded until the start of your next turn.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd or higher, the radius increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists. Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard

Fetid Cloud

2nd-level Conjuration

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S, M (a bit of rotten food)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You call forth a cloud of toxic vapors with a radius of 10 feet, centered on a point you choose within range. When a creature enters the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 3d4 poison damage on a failure, and half as much on a success. A creature is effected by this spell even if they hold their breath or do not need to breath.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists. Druid, Wizard

Disrupt Spell

1st-level Abjuration

Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 30 feet of you casting a spell
Range: 30 feet
Components: S
Duration: Instantaneous

You attempt to disrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell, increasing its drain on the caster. If the creature is casting a spell of 1st level or lower, that creature may expend an additional spell slot of 1st level or higher. If it does not, its spell fails and has no effect.

If you are using the spell points variant rule and successfully cause a spell to fail using this spell, the creature may expend an additional 2 spell points instead of a spell slot.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the interrupted spell can be of a level less than or equal to the level of the spell slot you used.

Spell Lists. Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard

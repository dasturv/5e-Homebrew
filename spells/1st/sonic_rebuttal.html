Sonic Rebuttal

1st-level Evocation Sonomancy

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 90 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

You produce a harsh sound concentrated on a creature of your choice within range. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 3d6 thunder damage, or half as much on a success. If this damage forces the target to make a concentration check, it does so at disadvantage.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists. Bard, Wizard

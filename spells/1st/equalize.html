Equalize

1st-level Evocation

Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself makes an ability check, an attack roll or a saving throw with either advantage or disadvantage
Range: 60 feet
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous

You call on the forces of order to expunge chaotic elements. The triggering creature's roll is not affected by advantage and disadvantage.

Spell Lists. Cleric, Wizard

Noxella

Noxella, goddess of the moon, the Silver Lady and second of the Radiant Trio. From her realm within Celestia she promotes gentle kindness, grace, empathy and resilience in the face of evil. She reaches out to aid the suffering and meek, and teaches her devout to do the same. Noxella does not care what race, age, upbringing or past her followers have, for all have the capacity for good in her name.

Cosmology

Noxella is old, and while she did not create the world, she has seen ages come and pass. To her followers, she is a timeless force of goodness, tirelessly working to save a broken world. It is believed that when a follower of Noxella, or even just a good bug not devoted to another god, dies, their soul goes to her moonlit realm in Celestia: a land of quiet song and tranquil silver streams. There the dead devout enjoy blissful peace forevermore.

Followers

Noxella and the rest of the Radiant Trio are not exclusive to any race, culture or region. Instead, their church spreads to wherever their message would take root. For Noxella, this means her strongest centers of worship lie within the Whistlewood amongst the Cuinin Amhrani and throughout the Abellid empire, with an exceptionally strong concentration in the Forsor's territory. Despite this, she is worshipped everywhere throughout Llorelan, from grand cathedrals to private shrines in her devout's homes.

Structure

Noxella's doctrine is predominantly spread through her church, where formalized education, priests, and bylaws hold sway. That being said, the church of Noxella is not afraid of sects popping up where her message is needed. This has led to Noxella's worship becoming a strange blend of the other two of the Radiant Trio, with small sects like Stellis and organized religion like Solaris. Though, while this hybrid approach has led to a lot of flexibility, it also provides tension between the more and less organized portions of her worshippers.

Factions

Unionists
Made up primarily by the central church of Noxella, the Unionists believe that while the sects of Noxella worship are good, they should aim to become part of the church structure over time. Citing fears over disunity, doctrine shift and even outright blasphemy, the Unionists work tirelessly to find and incorporate the disparate groups of Noxella's worshippers.

Fragment Sects
While not known by any specific name, many of the individual sects of Noxella worship resist become absorbed into the greater structure of Noxella's church. Oftentimes these sects form in places of great suffering, growing a following based on the tenets of Noxella that best fits the needs of the community. When the wider church steps in, many of these traits that made Noxella so appealing to those followers are stripped away or pushed from the forefront, oftentimes leading to resentment and even collapse.

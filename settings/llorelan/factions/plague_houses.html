The Profane Houses

Description

A loose collection of rival Amedruzcan noble houses and their serfs, the Plague Houses form a fell aristocracy within the Dreadmoor. Consantly scheming and infighting, only the awe and fear Maglataz inspires in them keeps them in any way united.

Culture

For the nobles of the Profane Houses, power is all that matters. Wealth, influence, martial prowess, divine favor; all sources of the power that keeps them in positions of comfort while everyone else suffers. Each house has their own unique way of amassing and keeping power for themselves, leveraging every tactic and advantage, no matter how unsavory. Nobles committing crimes are punished for being caught, not for the crime itself, with assassination, double dealing and betrayal being not just expected, but respected amongst the upper caste. The lower castes are not allotted such lenience, with any attempt to climb out of their status repressed brutally.

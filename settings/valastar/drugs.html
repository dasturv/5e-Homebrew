In a world where so many seek an escape, either from horrible living conditions or intense boredom in a cushioned life, drugs are all too common. Ranging from benign party drugs to life threatening combat stims, drugs in valastar serve a very similar purpose as potions in other worlds, though with far more dangerous side effects.

Obtaining Drugs
Drugs are spread across the entire breadth of legality, cost and rarity. The more illegal and more rare a drug, the harder it is to find and purchase. Legal drugs can be found in general stores, pharmacies and factory canteens throughout the city. Restricted drugs are more specialized, being sold by doctors and pharmacists and often require some form of prescription or registration to purchase and sell. Illegal drugs are only sold on black markets and through criminal contacts, and even being caught holding onto them can put you behind bars.

Doing Drugs
In order to gain the benefits of a drug, you need to introduce it to your system. Each drug has one or more Vectors, or methods to use it. These vectors can be contact, ingestion, inhalation, or injection. Each vector also has an Acting Time, or amount of time must pass after the vector method is used before the effects of the drug starts. After the acting time, a drug grants its effects. Finally, once the effects of the drug wears off, the user must make an addiction saving throw, as detailed below.

Traits: Gifted

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Flawed trait

You're just damn lucky. Like you got something watching out for you, random events just seem to occur exact when you need them to. You have a number of luck points equal to half of your proficiency bonus (rounded down). You regain all expended luck points when you complete a long rest.

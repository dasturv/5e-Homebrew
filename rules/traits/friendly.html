Traits: Friendly

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Abrasive trait

You have always been well liked. Maybe you just are a warm and friendly person, or maybe you were taught strict manners in a noble house. You gain a +5 bonus to the initial attitude scores of NPCs you interact with. If an NPC has a single attitude score for the whole party, you instead get a +1 bonus to the initial attitude score.

If you are not using the NPC attitudes variant rule, NPCs are considered to be friendly towards you when you first meet them.

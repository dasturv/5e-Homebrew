Traits: Restful

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Restless trait

You find it easy to relax. Perhaps you are used to roughing it, or maybe you just know how to relax despite your environment. Whenever you roll a hit die to recover hit points, you roll a die one size larger instead. For example, if you are a cleric, you roll a d10. If your hit die is a d12, you instead gain an additional hit point whenever you expend a hit die to regain hit points.

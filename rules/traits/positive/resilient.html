Traits: Resilient

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Fragile trait

You are tougher than the average customer. Perhaps you grew up in a tough neighborhood, or maybe you came from a far off and dangerous land. Either way, you know how to take a punch. Your hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to your character level when your gain this feature. Whenever you gain a level thereafter, your hit point maximum increases by an additional point.

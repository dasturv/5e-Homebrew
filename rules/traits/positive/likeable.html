Traits: Likeable

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Abrasive trait

You have always been well liked. Maybe you just are a warm and friendly person, or maybe you were taught strict manners in a noble house, either way people tend to give you the benefit of the doubt. You are considered to be one attitude level higher with NPCs than normal, to a maximum of beloved.

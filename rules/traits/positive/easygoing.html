Traits: Easygoing

Positive trait

Prequisites: must be using the lifestyles variant rule.

Incompatible with the Fussy trait.

You don't mind roughing it. Maybe you grew up poor and were taught things weren't so bad, or maybe you completed military training and find even muddy ground to be comfortable. You are considered to be one lifestyle higher for the purposes of lifestyle effects, to a maximum of aristocratic. For example, if you are supporting a comfortable lifestyle, you gain the benefits of a wealthy lifestyle.

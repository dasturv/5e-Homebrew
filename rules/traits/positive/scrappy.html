Traits: Scrappy

Positive trait

You are rough and tumble, always ready for a fight. Perhaps you were raised in a setting where brawls were common, or maybe you got picked on growing up and decided to make your bullies stop. You gain proficiency with improvised weapons. Additionally, when you hit a creature with an unarmed strike, you can deal 1d4 + your Strength modifier bludgeoning damage instead of the normal damage for an unarmed strike.

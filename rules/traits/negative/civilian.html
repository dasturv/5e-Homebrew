Traits: Civilian

Negative trait

Prequisites: proficiency with martial weapons

Incompatible with the Militaristic trait.

Despite your combative role you find yourself in now, you lack the proper training to use more deadly weaponry. Maybe you were a noncombatant dragged into this life, or maybe you simply struggled with the more complicated weaponry your instructors tried to teach you. You lose proficiency with martial weapons.

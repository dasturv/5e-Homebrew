Traits: Dull

Negative trait

Incompatible with the Talented trait

You've never really been known for your cunning. Maybe you were never given the opportunity to pursue an education, or perhaps you just found the few things you knew how to do was enough. Either way, you've ended up a bit less versatile than you probably should have. You lose proficiency in two skills of your choice.

Traits: Volatile

Negative trait

Prequisites: spellcasting and must be using the spell drain variant rule.

Your spellcasting is dangerous, even to yourself. Perhaps you wield a magic that is antithetical to your biology, or maybe your god seeks to make your power more costly. Whenever you fail a drain check by 5 or more, you take necrotic damage equal to the amount of drain your recieve. This damage cannot be reduced in any way.

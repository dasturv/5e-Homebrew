Traits: Fussy

Negative trait

Prequisites: must be using the lifestyles variant rule.

Incompatible with the Easygoing trait.

You can't stand subpar living conditions. Perhaps you were raised in a noble family with wealth and luxury, or maybe you are just picky. You are considered to be one lifestyle lower for the purposes of lifestyle effects, to a minimum of squalid. For example, if you are supporting a comfortable lifestyle, you gain no benefits or penalties (as if you were supporting the modest lifestyle).

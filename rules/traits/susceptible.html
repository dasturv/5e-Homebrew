Traits: Susceptible

Negative trait

You are weaker against a specific type of damage. Perhaps you have a severe reaction due to a lingering injury, or maybe you have a stronger than usual connection with an exotic bloodline and inherited some of their weaknesses. You gain a vulnerability to one of the following damage types: acid, bludgeoning, cold, fire, force, lightning, necrotic, piercing, poison, psychic, radiant, slashing or thunder. The Dungeon master must approve the vulnerability, as the chosen damage type should be common enough to be a substantial threat.

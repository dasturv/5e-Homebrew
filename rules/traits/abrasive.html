Traits: Abrasive

Negative trait

Incompatible with the Friendly trait

You have always been a bit on the uncouth side. Maybe you never learned your manners, or perhaps you are just oblivious to other people's needs. You gain a -5 penalty to the initial attitude scores of NPCs you interact with. If an NPC has a single attitude score for the whole party, you instead get a -1 penalty to the initial attitude score.

If you are not using the NPC attitudes variant rule, NPCs are considered to be unfriendly towards you when you first meet them.

Traits: Magical Knack

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Spell-Dumb trait

You have always had a knack for magic, regardless of the path you took in life. Choose a class: bard, cleric, druid, sorcerer, warlock or wizard. You learn one cantrip of your choice from that class's spell list.

In addition, choose one 1st level spell from the chosen class's spell list. Using this trait, you can cast that spell once at it's lowest level, and you must complete a long rest before you can cast the spell in this way again. You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have.

Your spellcasting ability for these spells is Charisma, Intelligence or Wisdom (choose when you select this trait).

Traits: Wealthy

Positive trait

Incompatible with the Indebted trait

Whether due to an affluent family, a rich inheritence, canny investments or a successful business, you have a fairly continuous stream of income. Whenever you level up, or when you create your character, you gain bonus gold. The amount of gp you gain is dependent on your level and can be found on the table below.

Level Bonus gp
1st-5th 50 gp
6th-11th 500 gp
12th-17th 2,500 gp
18th-20th 10,000 gp

Depending on the events of the story, you may not gain the bonus gold immediately upon level up. the extra gold represents the profits or interest gained while you were away, and you can grab your share whenever you return to the business or to a bank connected to your source of income.

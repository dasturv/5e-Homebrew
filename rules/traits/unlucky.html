Traits: Unlucky

Negative trait

Incompatible with the Lucky trait

You just never seem to catch a break. Like some cosmic force is messing with you, when something can go wrong, it will. The DM has one karma point, and can spend it to use one of the features below. The DM regains this karma point whenever you complete a long rest.

Advantage. Whenever a creature makes an attack roll against you, the DM can expend one karma point to allow that creature to make that roll with advantage.

Disadvantage. Whenever you make an ability check, attack roll or saving throw, the DM can expend one karma point to force you to make that roll with disadvantage.

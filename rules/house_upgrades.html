Additional Land

Preresiquites: There must be nearby land that can be bought.
Cost: 50 gp
Slots Required: -
Build Time: -
 You purchase some more land nearby your home. You gain 1 land slot.

Additional Room

Preresiquites: The base house must be a cottage or larger. You must have the yard upgrade
Cost: One fifth the cost of the base house.
Slots Required: 1 yard upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
Special: You can have a number of additional rooms equal to the base house's upgrade slots.
 You build an additional room into your house. The house gains 1 additional upgrade slot.

Altar

Cost: 200 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
 You have a specialized room for worshipping and praying to a diety. Whenever a cleric or paladin completes a short rest within the room, they can choose expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less that caster's highest spell slot level, and none of the slots can be of 6th or higher. Once this feature is used it cannot be used again for a week.

Arcane Study

Cost: 250 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
 You have a specialized room for practicing a wizard's arcane magics. Whenever a wizard copies a spell into their spellbook while inside of this room, the cost and time required are halved. Additionally, when a arcane trickster, eldritch knight or wizard completes a short rest within the room, they can choose expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less that caster's highest spell slot level, and none of the slots can be of 6th or higher. Once this feature is used it cannot be used again for a week.

Archery Range

Cost: 150 gp
Slots Required: 1 yard upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 week
 You have set up a range to practice archery. If you spend at least one hour training, you gain a Training die. This die is a d6 and lasts one week or until you expend it. Whenever you miss an attack with a ranged weapon roll you can expend the die and roll it, adding it to the total, and potentially turning that miss into a hit. You can only have one Training die at a time.

Ballroom

Cost: 250 gp
Slots Required: 2 upgrade slots
Build Time: 2 months
 You have built a grand room to host parties and gatherings. Using your ballroom you can host parties, when you do so select a lifestyle level. A party costs an amount equal to ten times the daily cost of the chosen lifestyle and takes one week of preparation for each level of lifestyle it is above wretched. A party can serve up to twenty guests, and lasts for 12 hours. During the party, you have advantage on Charisma checks when interacting with guests of the chosen lifestyle level or lower. Additionally, you gain a further +2 bonus on Charisma checks for each lifestyle level the party is above the guest.

Bedroom

Cost: One tenth the cost of the base house.
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 week
 You have another room made to accommodate sleeping. The house gains an additional 2 capacity. If the home is a hovel, the home gains only 1 capacity.

Clear Land

Preresiquites: You must have a land slot available.
Cost: 10 gp
Slots Required: 1 land slot
Build Time: 1 week
 You clear a section of land you own, preparing it for use. You gain one yard upgrade slot. Cleared land must be maintained, and each yard upgrade slot counts towards the total upgrades slots a house has for the purposes of maintanence. When a house falls into disrepair, and each subsequent month a house continues to be in disrepair, half of home's remaining yard slots become overgrown. An overgrown slot cannot be built upon, and any upgrades connected with that slot cannot be used while it is overgrown. If a home is condemned, all of its yard upgrade slots return to wilderness and are lost, along with any upgrades on them.

Common Room

Cost: One tenth the cost of the base house.
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 week
 You have a room made for eating and socializing. Without this room, a home's lifestyle level is reduced by one, to a minimum of squalid.

Demolish

Cost: -
Slots Required: -
Build Time: Half of the upgrade's build time
 You demolish one upgrade that you have built, losing it permanently. You regain the slots used on that upgrade.

Docks

Preresiquites: The house must have access to a large body of water or river. You must have the yard upgrade
Cost: 250 gp
Slots Required: 1 yard upgrade slot
Build Time: 6 months
 You build a set of docks on your property, allowing you access to the water. The docks can contain one warship, two sailing ships, two longships, four keelboats, or eight rowboats, provided the water way is deep enough. To contain a galley, you need two adjacent docks, which can contain one galley.

The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less that caster's highest spell slot level, and none of the slots can be of 6th or higher. Once this feature is used it cannot be used again for a week.

Game Room

Cost: 50 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 week
 You have a specialized room for playing a specific game. When you construct this upgrade, choose one game set. Checks made in this room with the chosen game gain a +5 bonus.

Guard Room

Cost: 200 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
 You have built a room specifically to house your guards, who help you protect your house. The room can house up to 4 guards. Each of those guards only cost 1 gp per day to hire. These guards do not count towards the home's capacity.

Gymnasium

Cost: 150 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 2 weeks
 You have built a room designed to train strength and agility. If you spend at least one hour training, you gain a Training die. This die is a d6 and lasts one week or until you expend it. Whenever you fail a Dexterity(Acrobatics) or Strength(Atheltics) check you can expend the die and roll it, adding it to the total, and potentially turning that failure into a success. You can only have one Training die at a time.

Holding Cells

Cost: 150 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
 You have built a room designed to contain and hold creatures. The room can hold up to four medium creatures in cells or cages. The cells can be opened with a DC 15 check with thieve's tools or a DC 25 Strength(Athletics) check. When you build this room you can pay and additional 50 gp to increase the DC for both checks by 2, to a maximum of 25 and 35 respectively. Furthermore, you can spend an additional 100 gp to ward the room, preventing the room from being targeted by divination magic, percieved through magical scrying sensor and prevents magical teleportation within its borders.

Insurance

Cost: One tenth the home's total cost
Slots Required: -
Build Time: -
 You have taken out insurance on your home. If your home is ever destroyed or rendered unlivable due to actions not caused by you (or cannot be proven to be caused by you), you can collect an amount of gold equal to half the total price of the home.

Interrogation Room

Cost: 150 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 2 weeks
 You have a room dedicated to interrogation or even torture. You have a +5 bonus to Wisdom(Insight) and Charisma(Persuasion) checks against other creatures in the room.

Library

Cost: 250 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 2 weeks
 You have a room dedicated to study and research. While in this room, you have a +5 bonus on Intelligence checks to research information on a topic.

Personalized Chamber

Cost: 200 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month

The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less that caster's highest spell slot level, and none of the slots can be of 6th or higher. Once this feature is used it cannot be used again for a week.

Recovery Room

Cost: 200 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
 You have set up a room to maximize the recovery speed of its inhabitants. A creature that spends at least 16 hours a day in the room while diseased or poisoned has advantage on saving throws to end that disease or poison on itself. In addition, whenever a creature expends hit dice to recover hit points while in the room, they may roll the hit die twice and use either number.

Secret Passages

Cost: 150 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 2 months
 You have installed several secret halls, tunnels and doorways throughout the house. You and anyone aware of the passages can use them to go between rooms within the house as well as enter or exit the house without being seen. To find a hidden passage requires a DC 20 Intelligence(Investigation) check.

Vault

Cost: 150 gp
Slots Required: 1 upgrade slot
Build Time: 1 month
 You have built a secure room to house your valuables or other items you wish protected. The room is a 10 feet cube and securely locked. An individual trying to enter the room without the proper code or key must make a DC 15 check with thieve's tools or a DC 20 Strength(Athletics) check. When you build this upgrade, you can spend an additional 50 gp to increase both DCs by 2, to a max of 25 and 35 respectively. Furthermore, you can spend an additional 100 gp to ward the room, preventing the room from being targeted by divination magic, percieved through magical scrying sensor and prevents magical teleportation within its borders.

 You own a small garden that grows produce. Your daily cost to maintain your lifestyle in the house is reduced by 1 sp, to a minimum of 0.

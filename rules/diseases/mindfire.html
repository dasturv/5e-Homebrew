Disease: Mindfire

Mindfire is a disease that is characterized by intense fevers and confusion in those who are suffering from it. While rarely dangerous, a few unfortunate souls can suffer long term effects that impair their ability to remember and use logic.

Infection

Mindfire is spread predominantly through inhalation. A non-infected humanoid has a 10% chance of being exposed each hour they spend within 20 feet of an infected creature. An exposed creature must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or become infected. A creature that wears a mask while at risk of exposure has advantage on this save.

Immunity Check: DC 60 Constitution Save (interval 1 day)

Stage 0 (Incubation)

Identify Check: DC 25 Wisdom (Medicine).

The disease progresses onto its next stage after 1d4 days.

Stage 1 (Early Symptoms)

Identify Check: DC 20 Wisdom (Medicine).

An infected creature at this stage gains a level of exhaustion.

The disease progresses onto its next stage after 1d4 days.

Stage 2 (Illness)

Identify Check: DC 15 Wisdom (Medicine).

An infected creature at this stage gains an additional level of exhaustion (for a total of 2). Additionally, the infected creature's Intelligence is reduced by 2 (minimum of 1).

The disease progresses onto its next stage after 1d6 days.

Stage 3 (Decline)

Identify Check: DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine).

An infected creature at this stage gains an additional level of exhaustion (for a total of 3). Additionally, the infected creature's Intelligence is reduced by 5 (minimum of 1).

The disease progresses onto its final stage after 1d6 days.

Stage 4 (Conclusion)

An infected creature at this stage gains an additional level of exhaustion (for a total of 4). Additionally, the infected creature's Intelligence is reduced to 1.

Recovery Scar. The creature has its Intelligence reduced by 2.

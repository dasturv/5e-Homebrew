Lifestyles: Travelling

In standard 5e DnD, there is not much incentive to take care of yourself. Why sleep in a castle when roughing it in a tent provides the same benefits? The following variant rule adds a bit more complexity to this system.

Inspiration: Shadowrun.

Travelling

Oftentimes you have to leave your home and lifestyle and travel, which is never as comfortable. While travelling you must have access to shelter, food, sleep and water. Below are several tables showing options for each of these categories. To calculate your lifestyle while travelling, select the lowest lifestyle level you have from all of the categories.

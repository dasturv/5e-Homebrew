Catalogue: Magic Items

Magic items are rare and powerful items that range from healing potions to legendary magical weapons. As such, the price range is just as intense.

Minor and Major Items
Magic items are seperated into minor items, which are consumables like potions and scrolls, and major items, which are permanent items that provide lasting benefits to a player.

Item Rarity
Magic Items each have a rarity, being Common, Uncommon, Rare, Very Rare or Legendary (Artifacts do not have a price).

Price Tiers
Additionally, when deciding a magic item's base price, you must decide on its tier. Working in the same way as a tier list, an item is closer to A if its benefits are nearly always applicable (health potions, +X weapons, arcane grimoires), and closer to F if its rarely useful or outclassed by other items in the same or lower rarity.

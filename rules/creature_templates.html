Creature Templates

In standard 5e DnD, there are very few creature templates. The following sections are designed to offer more options for diverse creatures.

Applying Templates

Templates can be applied to any creature, with the creature gaining all the features of said template, and its challenge rating (CR) increasing by a specified amount. A creature below CR 1 becomes the specified CR instead. Some features increase attribute scores. When this occurs, you may need to recalculate the creature's hit points, armor class, attack rolls, damage rolls and save DCs.

Air Elementals

Elusive

Elusive creatures have been touched by the primal element of air. Light and graceful, they grow both curious and quick to startle.

Attributes: Str +2, Dex +2
Movement: Each of the creature's movement speeds are increased by 10 feet.
CR: +2
Resistances: Thunder
Avoidance: If the creature is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.
Fleeting: This creature does not provoke attack of opportunities while moving.

Ephemeral

Ephemeral creatures have been deeply infused with primal air. Often looking mostly transparent or even made of clouds, they gracefully drift throughout the world.

Attributes: Str +4, Dex +4
Movement: Each of the creature's movement speeds are increased by 30 feet.
CR: +5
Immunities: Thunder
Gigantism: The creature's size is is increased by one size. Additionally, the creature's hit dice is increased by one dice size and its weapon attacks deal one additional damage die of damage. For example, a cave bear's claws would do 3d6 damage (instead of 2d6) and its bite would deal 2d8 (instead of 1d8).
Avoidance: If the creature is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.
Fleeting: The creature does not provoke attacks of opportunities while moving.
Fog Cover: The creature produces a fog in a 20 foot radius sphere centered on itself. The area is light obscured and ranged attacks that pass through the area are made at disadvantage.

Earth Elementals

Enduring

Enduring creatures have been touched by the primal element of stone. Their hide and scales harden, bones grow denser and the creature becomes more patient and slower to rouse.

Attributes: Str +2, Con +2
CR: +2
AC: +2
Resistances: Piercing, Slashing
Vulnerabilities: Bludgeoning
Siege Monster: The creature deals double damage to objects and structures.

Unyielding

Unyielding creatures have been deeply infused with primal stone. Often looking more like statues or boulders than living creatures, with personalities to match.

Attributes: Str +4, Con +4
CR: +5
AC: +4
Resistances: Piercing, Slashing
Vulnerabilities: Bludgeoning
Senses: Tremorsense 60 ft.
Gigantism: The creature's size is is increased by one size. Additionally, the creature's hit dice is increased by one dice size and its weapon attacks deal one additional damage die of damage. For example, a cave bear's claws would do 3d6 damage (instead of 2d6) and its bite would deal 2d8 (instead of 1d8).
Siege Monster: The creature deals double damage to objects and structures.
Tremors: Surfaces within 15 feet of the creature is considered difficult terrain for other creatures.

Fire Elementals

Smoldering

Smoldering creatures have been touched by the primal element of fire. Their colors brighten, they steam and smoke when agitated, and they become increasingly irritable.

Attributes: Str +2, Dex +2
CR: +2
Resistances: Fire
Aggressive: As a bonus action, the creature can move up to its speed towards a hostile creature.
Burning: The creature's weapon attacks deal an additional 2d4 fire damage.

Blazing

Blazing creatures have been deeply infused with primal fire. Often brightly colored and constantly ablaze, they constantly pick fights with whatever they come across.

Attributes: Str +4, Dex +4
CR: +5
Immunities: Fire
Gigantism: The creature's size is is increased by one size. Additionally, the creature's hit dice is increased by one dice size and its weapon attacks deal one additional damage die of damage. For example, a cave bear's claws would do 3d6 damage (instead of 2d6) and its bite would deal 2d8 (instead of 1d8).
Aggressive: As a bonus action, the creature can move up to its speed towards a hostile creature.
Eruption: When this creature is reduced to 0 hit points, it explodes. Each other creature within 20 feet of it must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 + proficiency bonus + constitution modifier), taking 3d10 fire damage on a failure or half as much on a success.
Scorching: This creatures weapon attacks deal an additional 2d6 fire damage.

Frost Elementals

Frigid

Frigid creatures have been touched by the primal element of ice. Lean and savage, they prowl desolate places as dangerous predators.

Attributes: Str +2, Con +2
CR: +2
Resistances: Cold
Freezing: The creature's weapon attacks deal an additional 1d8 cold damage. Whenever a creature takes this damage, its speed is reduced by 5 feet until the end of its next turn.
Opportunistic: The creature has advantage on attack rolls for opportunity attacks.

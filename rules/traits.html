Variant Rule: Traits

Standard DnD 5e's character creation process is incredibly deep... to a point. Compared to many other editions and systems, it is downright simple (as it was designed to be). However, using these rules, a bit more flavor and character can be built into player characters right form the start, granting both strengths and weaknesses to play around with.

Harish Traits

Regardless of what form they take, all Harish share the following traits.

  • Ability Score Increase. When determining your character's ability scores, increase one score by 2 and increase a different score by 1, or increase three different scores by 1. You can't raise any of your scores above 20.
  • Creature Type. You are an insectoid.
  • Size. Your size is medium. Harish tend to be between 5 and 7 sprigs tall, but can be nearly twice as long. You weigh up to 400 pebblets.
  • Speed. Your walking speed is 30 sprigs.
  • Burst of Speed. You can use your many legs to move quicker than usual. As a bonus action you can move up to your speed. During this movement, attacks made against you are made with disadvantage. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you complete a long rest.
  • Natural Weapons. Your fanged maw and constricting body are natural weapons, which you can use to make unarmed strikes. If you hit with your bite, you deal piercing damage equal to 1d4 + your Strength modifier, and your target must make a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier). On a failed save, the target takes 1d4 poison damage.

  • Poison Resistance. You have resistance to poison damage and have advantage on saving throws to prevent or end the poisoned condition on yourself.
  • Martial Prowess. You have proficiency with one martial weapon of your choice.
  • Languages. Your character can speak, read and write Click-clack and Harish. Harish is a rigid language full of guttural growls and clicks.

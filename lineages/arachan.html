Arachan (Spiders)
The great spider pounced forwards towards the pair, the mother clutching her child in mindless fear. Suddenly there was a flash of light as an Arachan appeared above it, using gravity and his weight to stab a spear into the beasts thrashing head. It roared and gargled before sliding to a stop, dead. The pair silently looked up to their savior. He tried to smile. The mother screamed and ran, dragging her child behind her. The Arachan sighed. "At least they are safe," he thought to himself...

Born of Darkness

Every Arachan is touched by the foul magic of the Demonweb Pits, a realm of darkness and nightmares. The horrifying demonic Spiders of this realm crawl from the darkest corners of the world to prey upon all of bug-kind. Every Arachan shares a bloodline with these Spiders, and a deep malicious hunger rests deep within each, whether they fight it or embrace it.

Hushed Outcasts

To have an Arachan born within a community is considered a mark of some great sin. As such, most are hidden away or cast out from their families and communities. These Arachan are forced to grow up fast and learn how to survive in a world that neither loves nor trusts them.

Bitter Hardship

Most Arachan die young, alone and afraid. Cast out from their communities and shunned by the world, the few who learn to survive do so on their own or are taught by a more experienced Arachan or other mentor. facing such hardships, the few who make it in the world show a resolve truly rare amongst other bugs, with impossible odds merely being a fact of life for the Arachan.

Misfits and Vagabonds

Arachan come quite easily to the adventuring life. Whether through selfish greed or simply trying to help the world in spite of the intolerance they face, many Arachan's only family is that of an group of adventurers.

Arachan Names

While most Arachan do have a birth name, most never use it as it reminds them of a painful past. Instead, most Arachan choose their own name, whether to intimidate or put others at ease.

Lineage Names: Bolyuth, Candy, Hunter, Magleth, Peace, Venom, Virtue, Weaver

Arachan Traits

Regardless of form, all Arachan have the following traits.

  • Ability Score Increase. When determining your character's ability scores, increase one score by 2 and increase a different score by 1, or increase three different scores by 1. You can't raise any of your scores above 20.
  • Creature Type. You are an insectoid.
  • Size. Your size is medium or small. You choose when you select this lineage. An Arachan's height and weight are dependent on their parent's lineage.
  • Speed. Your walking speed is 30 sprigs.
  • Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 sprigs of you as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.
  • Foul Resistance. You have resistance to poison damage.
  • Otherwordly Presence. You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip.
  • Spider Climbing. You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed. Additionally, you can't be caught in webs of any sort and can move through webs as if they were difficult terrain.
  • Subrace. You choose a subrace and gain its features. Choose one: Corrupting, Ensnaring or Predatory.
  • Languages. Your character can speak, read and write Click-clack and Arachan. Arachan is a foul language made up of clicks and hisses and tends to be unsettling to other bugfolk.
Corrupting

Corrupting Arachans have strong affinities for diseases and poisons, using their powers to weaken and debilitate their prey.

  • Corruptive Magic. You know the Poison Spray cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Ray of Sickness. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Blindness/Deafness. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
Ensnaring

Ensnaring Arachans use abilities that trap others, savoring the fear their webs and nets instill in their prey.

  • Ensnaring Magic. You know the Sapping Sting cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Entangle. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Web. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
Predatory

Predatory Arachans enjoy the feeling of the hunt, stalking and chasing their prey down and getting close and personal.

  • Predatory Magic. You know the Primal Savagery cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Hunter's Mark. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Invisibility. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.

Caelin (Butterflies)
Grik didn't know what was happening. Just a moment ago the big bandit was shoving him into the ground at knife point, now all he heard was screams. He kept his head down, bright lights flashing all around him. After a beat, there was silence, and a hand touched his shoulder. He looked up to see the kind eyes of a bug, their wings a wave of dancing colors...

Born of Light

Every Caelin owes their existence to the realm of Celestia, a world of primordial good and light. It is said that the light of this plane comes forth into the material through the sun and moon, an idea supported by the dual types of Caelin, Lunar and Solar, who each exemplify different aspects of goodness.

Independent Souls

A Caelin can be born amongst the members of any race, to any parents. The birth of a Caelin is often considered a sacred event, with the child being assumed to have some great destiny or mission. As such, when they come to age, a Caelin often sets out into the world in pursuit of their fated destiny.

Embodiments of Virtue

Caelin tend towards the extremes of good. Zealous warriors seeking out evil, patient healers always looking to forgive and charitable freedom fighters facing off against tyrants are all commonplace amongst their numbers. A Caelin is devoted to their personal brand of good, and seek to spread it far and wide.

Pilgrims and Avengers

For a Caelin, the adventuring life comes easy. The forces of evil are powerful and widespread, and one does not combat the darkness with idleness. Whether seeking to bring comfort to the suffering or defeat some evil force, virtually every Caelin finds themself on some sort adventure.

Caelin Names

Most Caelin take a name from their parent's lineage and culture, though many also choose names that help exemplify their mission or goals.

Caelin Names: Faith, Freedom, Grace, Hope, Mercy, Light, Order, Wisdom

Caelin Traits

Regardless of form, all Caelin have the following traits.

  • Ability Score Increase. When determining your character's ability scores, increase one score by 2 and increase a different score by 1, or increase three different scores by 1. You can't raise any of your scores above 20.
  • Creature Type. You are an insectoid.
  • Size. Your size is medium or small. You choose when you select this lineage. A Caelin's height and weight are dependent on their parent's lineage.
  • Speed. Your walking speed is 30 sprigs.
  • Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 sprigs of you as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.
  • Otherwordly Presence. You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip.
  • Wings. Starting at 5th level, you can use a bonus action to magically strengthen your wings for 1 minute. For the duration, you gain a fly speed equal to your walking speed and can hover. Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until yout complete a long rest.
  • Subrace. You choose a subrace and gain its features. Choose either Lunar or Solar.
  • Languages. Your character can speak, read and write Click-clack and Caelin. Caelin is a light language made up of airy syllables and whistles.
Lunar

Lunar Caelin are gentle souls who seek to heal the sick and protect the weak. Their wings tend to have muted colors and softer, fuzzy textures.

  • Lunar Magic. You know the Guidance cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Cure Wounds. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Lesser Restoration. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
  • Lunar Resistance. You have resistance to necrotic damage.
Solar

Solar Caelin are heroic folks who seek out evil to destroy in the name of good. Their wings tend to be bright and aggressively colored.

  • Solar Magic. You know the Sacred Flame cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Divine Favor. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Zone of Truth. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
  • Solar Resistance. You have resistance to radiant damage.

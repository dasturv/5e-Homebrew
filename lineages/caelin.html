Caelin (Butterflies)

Caelin Traits

Regardless of form, all Caelin have the following traits.

  • Ability Score Increase. When determining your character's ability scores, increase one score by 2 and increase a different score by 1, or increase three different scores by 1. You can't raise any of your scores above 20.
  • Creature Type. You are an insectoid.
  • Size. Your size is medium or small. You choose when you select this lineage. A Caelin's height and weight are dependent on their parent's lineage.
  • Speed. Your walking speed is 30 sprigs.
  • Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 sprigs of you as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.
  • Otherwordly Presence. You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip.
  • Celestial Wings. Starting at 5th level, you can use a bonus action to magically strengthen your wings for 1 minute. For the duration, you gain a fly speed equal to your walking speed and can hover. Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until yout complete a long rest.
  • Subrace. You choose a subrace and gain its features. Choose either Lunar or Solar.
  • Languages. Your character can speak, read and write Click-clack and Caelin. Caelin is a light language made up of airy syllables and whistles.
Lunar

Lunar Caelin are gentle souls who seek to heal the sick and protect the weak. Their wings tend to have muted colors and softer, fuzzy textures.

  • Lunar Magic. You know the Guidance cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Cure Wounds. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Lesser Restoration. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
  • Lunar Resistance. You have resistance to necrotic damage.
Solar

Solar Caelin are heroic folks who seek out evil to destroy in the name of good. Their wings tend to be bright and aggressively colored.

  • Lunar Magic. You know the Sacred Flame cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Divine Favor. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Zone of Truth. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
  • Lunar Resistance. You have resistance to necrotic damage.

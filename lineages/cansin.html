Cansin

Lineage Traits

Beings touched by primal chaos, Cansin tend to be reckless and spontaneous. Their appearance varies wildly, and often times changes seemingly at random. Odd colors, proportions and markings are all common to the Cansin. All Cansin share the following traits.

  • Ability Score Increase. When determining your character's ability scores, increase one score by 2 and increase a different score by 1, or increase three different scores by 1. You can't raise any of your scores above 20.
  • Creature Type. You are a humanoid.
  • Size. Your size is medium or small. you choose the size when you select this lineage.
  • Speed. Your walking speed is 30 feet.
  • Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light. You discern colors in that darkness only as shades of gray.
  • Protean Resistance. Whenever you complete a long rest, roll twice on the Protean Resistance table below and choose one. You gain resistance to the chosen damage type until you roll on the table again.
  • Otherwordly Presence. You know the Thaumaturgy cantrip.
  • Legacy of Chaos. You know the Chill Touch cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level you can cast Chaos Bolt. Once you reach 5th level, you can also cast Crown of Madness. Once you cast either spell with this trait, you cannot cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest; however, you can cast the spell using any Spell Slots you have of the appropriate level.
    • Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait. You choose which ability when you choose this lineage.
  • Languages. Your character can speak, read and write Common and Inevitable.

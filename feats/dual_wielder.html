Dual Wielder (Rebalanced)

You master fighting with two weapons, gaining the following benefits:

  • You gain a +1 bonus to AC while you are wielding a separate melee weapon in each hand.
  • Whenever a creature within your reach misses you with a melee attack, you may make an off-hand attack against that creature provided you have a light weapon in each hand. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you recover any expended uses when you complete a long rest.
  • You can draw or stow two one-handed weapons when you would normally be able to draw or stow only one.

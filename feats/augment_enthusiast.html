Augment EnthusiastVala

You have taken a liking to augmenting yourself, learning to interface with them better. You gain the following benefits:

  • Your maximum neural load is increased by 2.
  • You have advantage on Constitution saving throws for deciding if your body rejects a newly installed augment.
  • You no longer recieve any penalties to your spellcasting or ki attack bonuses or save DCs for having augmentations.

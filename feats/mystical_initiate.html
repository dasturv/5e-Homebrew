Mystical Initiate

You learn one psionic talent and one psionic discipline available to the mystic class.

If you do not have the psionics class feature, you have 2 psi points and a psi limit of 2. You can spend psi points on psionic disciplines, following the rules for psionics (as shown in the mystic class,) Intelligence is your psionic ability. You recover all spent psi points when you complete a long rest.

If you have the psionics class feature, you instead gain 2 psi points. Your psi limit is unaffected.

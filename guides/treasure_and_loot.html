Guide: Treasure and Loot

Vanilla DnD 5e has quite loose guidelines when it comes to treasure such as magic items, gold and other forms of loot, and begins to fall apart quite quickly when scrutinized. The following guide seeks to remedy the issues of the default system using more realistic treasure amounts and a more clear way to spread loot throughout a campaign.

Guide: Making Encounters

Encounters are moments in a session where you switch from narration to interaction between the players and the game world. While usually combative in nature, encounters also include traps, puzzles and tense social situations. Anything that provides a significant barrier to the party and their goals could be used as an encounter. Encounters are also the primary way that players gain experience points and progress their character.

Adaptive Analysis

When the target of your Critical Analysis feature is reduced to 0 hit points, you can use your reaction to analyze a target within 60 feet of you using your Critical Analysis feature, provided you aren't incapacitated.

Anatomical Expert

Prerequisite: 5th level, proficiency with Medicine or surgeon's tools

Your studies into biology and anatomy have granted you insights on where your foes are weakest. Your weapon attacks score a critical hit on the target of your Critical Analysis feature on a roll of 19 or 20.

Broadened Studies

Prerequisite: 3rd level

Choose a Scholar's pursuit that you are not a part of. You can replace one maneuver you know with a maneuver from the chosen pursuit. Whenever you are able to choose a new maneuver or replace a maneuver, you can choose maneuvers from the chosen pursuit.

You can make this discovery multiple times, choosing a new pursuit each time.

Calculated Odds

Prerequisite: proficiency with a game set

You are well versed in probabilities. When you roll a 1 on an ability check or saving throw, you can reroll the die. You must use the new result, even if it is a 1.

Careful Maintenance

Prerequisite: proficiency with smith's tools

As part of a long rest you can hone and sharpen a non-magical weapon to near perfection, provided you have access to smith's tools. For the next 24 hours, that weapon has a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

At later levels: starting at 11th level, you can instead choose a non-magical set of armor or shield. For the next 24 hours, that armor or shield grants a +1 bonus to AC while worn or held.

Clever Applications

Choose a skill that you are proficient in. Whenever you make an ability check with the chosen skill, you can choose to use your Intelligence modifier instead of the regular Ability Score modifier associated with that skill.

You can make this discovery multiple times.

Contingency Plan

Prerequisite: 11th level

When the target of your Critical Analysis feature attacks you and scores a critical hit, you can use your reaction and expend a superiority die to treat the attack as a normal hit instead.

Contortionist

Prerequisite: proficiency with Acrobatics

As an action you can escape from nonmagical restraints, such as manacles or a creature that has you grappled.

Discerning Eyes

You have advantage on saving throws against illusions and on Intelligence(Investigation) checks to discern them from reality.

Deep Analysis

Whenever you successfully analyze a creature using your Critical Analysis feature, you can glean extra information about the target's strengths and weaknesses. The DM tells you one of the following characteristics of your choice:

  • Ability with the Highest Score
  • Ability with the Lowest Score
  • Any Creature Type(s)
  • Any Damage Immunities
  • Any Damage Resistances
  • Any Damage Vulnerabilities

Deepened Studies

You learn a new maneuver of your choice, provided you meet it's requirements. This maneuver does not count against the number of maneuvers you know.

You can make this discovery multiple times, choosing a new maneuver each time.

Developed Resistance

Prerequisite: proficiency with poisoner's kits

You gain resistance to poison damage and have advantage on saving throws against poison.

Dungeoneer

Prerequisite: proficiency withthieve's tools

You have advantage on Wisdom(Perception) and Intelligence(Investigation) checks to locate any secret doors or traps.

Excellent Navigator

Prerequisite: proficiency with cartographer's tools or navigator's tools

You can no longer get lost except by magical means. Additionally, you always know which way is north.

Expert Demolitionist

Prerequisite: proficiency with mason's tools

You deal double damage to objects and structures that are analyzed by your Critical Analysis feature.

Extra Pockets

Prerequisite: proficiency with weaver's tools

You have advantage on Dexterity(Stealth) checks to hide items on your person.

Additionally, your Strength score is considered 4 higher for the purposes of determining your carrying capacity.

Fortified Construction

Prerequisite: proficiency with carpenter's tools or woodcarver's tools

You can increase the resilience of wooden objects, such as chest, doors, sections of wooden walls and carriages. The object being fortified cannot exceed 10 feet on any side. Fortifying an object takes one hour, and requires access to wood or other similar materials. A fortified object has a +2 bonus to its AC and has its hit points maximum doubled.

Hardened Mind

Prerequisite: 11th level

You gain resistance to psychic damage. Additionally, you gain advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.

Healing Elixirs

Prerequisite: proficiency with herbalism kits

As part of a long rest, you can create a number of healing elixirs equal to your proficiency bonus, provided you have access to an herbalism kit. As an action, a creature can consume an elixir to regain 1d4+2 hit points. An elixir loses its potency after 24 hours, becoming a useless slurry of herbal compounds. These elixirs count as Potions of Healing for the purposes of class features.

Insightful Teacher

Whenever you use your Sudden Insight feature, you can choose to target another creature instead of yourself. Choose a creature within 30 feet who can hear and understand you. Choose a skill or tool that creature lacks proficiency in. for the next minute, that creature has proficiency in the chosen skill or tool.

Jury-Rigger

Prerequisite: proficiency with tinker's tools

You are well versed in impromptu repairs. Over a period of one minute, you can repair a broken non-magical object to working order, provided the majority of the object is intact, and you have access to both materials and tinker's tools. An object repaired this way has 1 hit point and functions as normal for one hour or until it either takes or deals damage. A jury-rigged object that is destroyed cannot be repaired again using this feature until it is first completely repaired.

Life-long Learning

You gain proficiency in any combination of two skills and/or tools.

You can make this discovery multiple times.

Linguist

You learn two additional languages.

You can make this discovery multiple times.

Magical Studies

Prerequisite: proficiency with Arcana or Religion

Choose a class: bard, cleric, druid, sorcerer, warlock or wizard. You learn two cantrips of your choice from that class's spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for those spells.

You can make this discovery multiple times, choosing a different class each time.

Mastercraft

Prerequisite: proficiency with any artisan tools

The time it takes and the cost to craft an item using a tool with which you have proficiency is reduced by 20 percent for you.

Master Appraiser

Prerequisite: proficiency with jeweler's tools or painters's tools

You are an expert on valuable objects. The base price of gems and art objects you sell are 20% higher.

Nutritional Cooking

Prerequisite: proficiency with cook's utensils

As part of a long rest you can prepare an extra nutritional meal, provided you have access to cook's utensils and ingredients. Choose a number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus. Each of those creature's hit point maxiumum and current hit points increase by your Intelligence modifier until they complete a long rest.

Physical Conditioning

Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.

Reasoned Vigilance

You may add your Intelligence modifier to your Initiative rolls, provided you don't already add it to your initiative rolls.

Reflexive Analysis

When you make an initiative roll, you can use your reaction to analyze a target within 60 feet of you using your Critical Analysis feature, provided you aren't incapacitated.

Shatter Points

Prerequisite: proficiency with glassblower's tools or potter's tools

Your work with fragile materials has taught you how to find potential points of failure. When you score a critical hit on the target of your Critical Analysis feature with a weapon attack, you can roll the weapon's damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.

Signature Maneuver

Prerequisite: 11th level

When you make this discovery, choose a maneuver that you know. When you would expend a superiority die on the chosen maneuver, you can choose to instead use the highest number possible on that die instead of rolling it. The superiority die is not expended. Once you use this feature you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest.

Sound Dampening

Prerequisite: proficiency with leatherworker's tools

As part of a long rest you can use scraps of leather to muffle the sounds of a set of armor, provided you have access to leatherworker's tools. For the next 24 hours, that set of armor does not impose disadvantage to Dexterity(Stealth) checks.

At later levels: starting at 11th level, that armor instead grants advantage on Dexterity(Stealth) checks.

Specialist Footwear

Prerequisite: proficiency with cobbler's tools

As part of a long rest you can upgrade a number of pairs of shoes or boots equal to half your proficiency bonus (rounded down), provided you have access to cobbler's tools. For each pair, choose an option below. For the next 24 hours, a creature wearing one of the pairs gains its bonus effect, provided the footwear is designed for creatures such as themselves and fit properly (A dragonborn couldn't wear shoes made for a halfling, for example.)

Cleats. A creature wearing these shoes has advantage on saving throws to prevent being pushed, pulled or knocked prone.

Hiking Boots. A creature wearing these shoes ignore the effects of ground based, non-magical difficult terrain, such as loose rocks, deep snow and roots.

Sprinting Shoes. A creature wearing these shoes increases its speed by 5 feet.

Stout Brew

Prerequisite: proficiency with brewer's supplies

As part of a long rest you can brew and distill an extra potent beverage, provided you have access to brewer's supplies. You make a number of stout brews equal to your proficiency bonus. As an action, a creature can consume an brew to gain temporary hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier. Additionally, for the next minute, you have advantage on Charisma(Persuasion) checks against that creature. A Brew loses its potency after 24 hours, becoming a sour and unappetizing.

Subliminal Messages

Prerequisite: proficiency with calligrapher's tools or forgery kit's

You know how to hide a message in plain sight, using the knowledge of others to ensure the message is only interpreted by the chosen few. While writing a message, you can choose to create a hidden message within it. The hidden message is one tenth the length of the original message. When you create a hidden message, choose a number of creatures equal to your intelligence modifier. Those creatures can decipher the hidden message easily, no check required. All other creatures looking for hidden messages must succeed an Intelligence(Investigation) check to find and decipher it. The Dc of this check is equal to 20 plus your intelligence modifier.

Targeted Analysis

Prerequisite: 14th level

Whenever you make an attack roll against the target of your Critical Analysis feature with disadvantage, you ignore that disadvantage instead.

Wondrous Physick

Prerequisite: proficiency with alchemist's supplies

As part of a long rest you can create a physick, provided you have access to alchemist's supplies. Choose one of the options below for your physick. As an action, a creature can consume the physick, gaining its benefits for 1 minute. A psysick loses its potency after 24 hours, becoming a useless slurry of alchemical compounds.

Dulling Physick. At the start of each of its turns, a creature under the effects of this physick gains 1d4 temporary hit points.

Fortifying Physick. A creature under the effects of this physick gains a +2 bonus to its AC.

Invigorating Physick. While under the effects of this physick, a creature's weapon attacks deal an additional 1d4 damage.

Hasty Physick. A creature under the effects of this physick increases its speed by 10 feet.

At later levels: starting at 11th level, whenever you make a physick you can choose two physick options. The created physick applies the effects of both selected options simultaneously.

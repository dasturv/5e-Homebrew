Artificer: Archivist

Information is a powerful thing, though hard to collect and store. For ages, song and stories held knowledge, then it was books and scrolls, but the first Archivists imagined something even greater: storing information within an artificial mind. What library could match a psychic archive capable of accessing, sorting and storing information in seconds? Archivists are those on the cutting edge of magical science, masters of creating artificial intelligences fueled by magic.

Tool Proficiency

When you adopt this specialization at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with calligrapher's supplies. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan's tools of your choice.

Artificial Mind

Starting at 3rd level, you discover how to awaken an intelligence within an object. Whenever you complete a long rest while you have access to your calligrapher's supplies (or other applicable artisan tool), you can magically rouse a keen mind within a tiny, nonmagical object. When your create the artificial mind, you choose its temperment, which can be Charming, Patient or Pragmatic. While instilled with the mind, the object is magical and you can use it as a spellcasting focus. If you already have an artificial mind created using this feature, the first one immediately dissipates. The magic item has the following properties:

Telepathic Advisor
The artificial mind telepathically supplies you with advice and information on certain topics. When you complete a long rest or create a new artificial mind, you may choose two skills from the Skill Proficiencies column associated with your Artificial Mind's temperment (see the Artificial Mind Temperments table below). While the mind is on your person you have proficiency with the chosen skills.

Manifest Mind
As an action while the magic item is on your person, you can cause the mind within it to manifest, appearing as a tiny spectral form above you. You decide the presence's appearence, with the Artificial Mind Temperments table offering some ideas. The presence is intangible and invulnerable, and moves with you. While it is manifested you shed dim light in a 10-foot radius and gain additional benefits based on your Artificial Mind's temperment:

  • Charming. You have advantage on Charisma checks and saving throws. Additionally, when a creature fails the saving throw for your Information Overload feature, the next attack roll made against that creature before the end of your next turn is made with advantage.
  • Patient. You have advantage on Wisdom checks and saving throws. Additionally, whenever a creature fails the saving throw for your Information Overload feature, that creature cannot take reactions until the end of your next turn.
  • Pragmatic. You have advantage on Intelligence checks and saving throws. Additionally, the damage of your Information Overload feature is increased by 1d8.

The manifestation lasts for one minute. It ends early if you become incapacitated or use an action to end the effect. Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest. Starting at 9th level, you can also expend a spell slot of 3rd level or higher to use this feature again.

Information Overload

Also at 3rd level, you gain the ability to psychically overload and distract your opponents. As a bonus action while your artificial mind is on your person, you can force a creature you can see within 90 feet of you to make an intelligence saving throw. On a failed save the target takes 1d8 psychic damage and the next attack roll they make before the start of your next turn is made with disadvantage.

This damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 9th level (3d8) and 15th level (4d8).

Mind Network

At 5th level your knowledge on minds has unlocked new potential options to both communicate and harm. You gain the following bonuses:

  • While your artificial mind is on your person, you can communicate telepathically with anyone carrying an item bearing one of your artificer infusions. That person can also communicate telepathically with you as long as they have that item. This communication can occur over any distance, but only while the creature is on the same plane of existence as you.
  • Whenever you deal psychic damage with an artificer spell or your Information Overload feature, you deal additional psychic damage equal to your intelligence modifier.

Empowered Mind

Starting at 9th level, you are able to pour even more magical potency into your artificial mind. When you select the skills for your Telepathic Advisor feature, you may select three skills from the list instead of two. Additionally, the bonuses provided while your Artificial Mind is manifested expand, based on its temperment:

  • Charming. When a creature fails the saving throw for your Information Overload feature, all attack rolls made against it before the end of your next turn are made with advantage.
  • Patient. Whenever a creature fails the saving throw for your Information Overload feature, that creature may either take a bonus action or action on its next turn, but not both.
  • Pragmatic. The damage of your Information Overload feature is further increased by 1d8, to a total bonus of 2d8.

Pure Information

By 15th level you are capable of using your artificial mind to tranform yourself into pure information. While your Artificial Mind is manifested, you gain the following benefits:

  • You have resistance to non-magical bludgeoning, piercing and slashing damage.
  • You can move through creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. If you end your turn inside of an object or creature you are returned to the last unnoccupied space you were in and take 2d10 force damage. This damage cannot be reduced.

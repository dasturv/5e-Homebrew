Artificer: Alchemist

An Alchemist is an expert at combining reagents to produce mystical effects. Alchemists use their creations to give life and to leech it away. Alchemy is the oldest of artificer traditions, and its versatility has long been valued during times of war and peace.

Tool Proficiency

When you adopt this specialization at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with alchemist's supplies. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan's tools of your choice.

Replicate Potions

Starting at 3rd level, you are able to create elixirs that mimic the effects of potions. Whenever you complete a long rest, you are able to create up to two elixirs, provided you have access to your alchemist's supplies. An elixir is a magical fluid stored within a tiny vessel. You can have a maximum of two elixirs. If you attempt to make an elixir while you already have the maximum number of them, the oldest elixir turns into non-magical sludge, no longer counting against your maximum number of elixirs.

When you make an elixir, select a potion from the Replicable Potions tables. The table's title tells you what level you must be in the class to choose a potion from the table. An elixir copies all the effects of the selected potion. After being used the elixir becomes non-magical and no longer counts against your maximum number of elixirs.

Restorative Reagents

At 5th level your mastery of magical chemicals enhance your abilities to heal. Whenever one of your elixirs or a spell you cast would cause one or more creatures to recover hit points, those creatures recover bonus hit points equal to your proficiency bonus.

Alchemical Savant

Starting at 9th level, your alchemical experiments have provided you with the following benefits:

  • The maximum number of elixirs you can create as part of a long rest and have at one time each increase to three.
  • You gain resistance to acid damage and poison damage, and you are now immune to the poisoned condition.

Grand Elixirs

By 15th level you have become unparalled in your crafting of elixirs. Whenever you would create one or more elixirs, you may choose to also create a grand elixir. A grand elixir is an elixir, but does not count against the number of elixirs you can have. When creating a grand elixir, you may choose a potion to replicate from the Grand Elixirs table below. You may only have one grand elixir at a time, and attempting to create a second one causes the first to turn into non-magical sludge.

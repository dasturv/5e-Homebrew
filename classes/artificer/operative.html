Artificer: Operative

An Operative is a master of surveillance and handling covert missions. Using techno-arcane means to gather information and coordinate allies, they provide a strong edge in any mission they are involved in.

Tool Proficiency

When you adopt this specialization at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with thieves' tools. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one type of artisan's tools of your choice.

Arcane Surveillance Network

Starting at 3rd level, you gain the ability to create discreet, magical sensors to observe and coordinate your allies.

As an action, you can create a Tiny, invisible magical sensor in an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet of you or one of your other sensors. The sensor is intangible and immune to damage, has senses equivalent to your own, and is detectable by magic such as see invisibility or detect magic. The sensor lasts for 1 hour or until you dismiss it (no action required). You can have a number of active sensors equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum 1). Attempting to create a new sensor while at this limit removes the oldest sensor.

As a bonus action you can tap into your network and do one of the following:

  • You percieve through one of your sensors until the start of your next turn. During this time you can see and hear from the sensor's position, but you are blind and deaf to your own senses.
  • You gain advantage on one Wisdom(Perception), Intelligence(Investigation) or Wisdom(Insight) check against a target or in a space one or more of your sensors can see.

Whenever one of your sensors would see or hear a creature (using the equivalent of your senses and passive Perception), you become aware that the sensor can see or hear something. This does not relay any additional information beyond the creature's presence.

Communication Network

Also at 3rd level, you can use your network to enable silent communication with your team. You can communicate telepathically with any creature within 60 feet of you or one of your sensors. You can allow those creatures to respond telepathically while within range.

Coordinated Strike

At 5th level you gain the ability to provide tactical insight to your allies and yourself using your network. Whenever you or an ally would make an attack roll against a target you can see within 15 feet of one or more of your sensors, you may do one of the following:

  • The attack roll gains a bonus equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of +1).
  • If the attack hits, it deals bonus damage equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of +1).

Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until the start of your next turn.

Expanded Network

Starting at 9th level, you become more efficient in using your Arcane Surveillance Network:

  • The range in which you can place your sensors increases to 60 feet, and the range of your Coordinated Strike feature increases to 30 feet.
  • Whenever you use your Coordinated Strike feature, you can choose both options instead of just one.

Perfect Coordination

By 15th level your battlefield awareness allows you to turn what would be complete failure into success. Whenever a creature you can see within 60 feet you or one of your sensors would miss an attack roll, fail a saving throw or ability check, you may use your reaction to allow that creature to reroll the d20 and gain a bonus to the roll equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of +1), potentially turning a miss into a hit or a failure into a success. The creature must use the new roll.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once), and regain all expended uses whenever you complete a long rest.

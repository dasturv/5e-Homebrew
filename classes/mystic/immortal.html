Mystic: Immortal

Mystics who dedicate themselves to the Order of the Immortal seek to infuse and enhance their bodies using their psionic powers. Capable of altering their forms, manipulating objects with their minds and forming solid barriers of psychic energy, the immortals are mighty and cunning warriors.

Bonus Disciplines

You learn bonus disciplines associated with your order. When you select this order at 1st level and again at 10th level, choose a psionic discipline from the Order Disciplines table to learn. These disciplines do not count against the number of disciplines you can know.

Order Disciplines
Alter Form
Celerity
Psionic Weapon
Psychic Barrier
Telekinetic Force

Bonus Proficiencies

Starting at 1st level when you choose this order, you gain proficiency with one of the following skills (your choice): Acrobatics, Athletics, Medicine, Perception.

Battle Trance

By 6th level you have improved your capabilities of weaving your martial and psionic abilities together. While in a trance using your psychic trance feature, you gain the following benefits:

  • When you enter the trance, you gain temporary hit points equal to twice your psi limit. These temporary hit points disappear when the trance ends.
  • Whenever you spend psi points on a psionic discipline or use a psionic talent on your turn, you can make a weapon attack as a bonus action.

Psionic Strikes

At 9th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with psionic energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 psychic damage to the target.

When you reach 17th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

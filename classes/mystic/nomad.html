Mystic: Nomad

Mystics who dedicate themselves to the Order of the Nomad seek enlightenment in all corners of the world. Masters of travel, both seen and unseen, the nomads wander the world collecting wisdom and knowledge wherever they go.

Bonus Disciplines

You learn bonus disciplines associated with your order. When you select this order at 1st level and again at 10th level, choose a psionic discipline from the Order Disciplines table to learn. These disciplines do not count against the number of disciplines you can know.

Order Disciplines
Knowledge Tap
Obfuscate
Psionic Arrow
Psychic Step
Third Eye

Bonus Proficiencies

Starting at 1st level when you choose this order, you gain proficiency with one of the following skills (your choice): Acrobatics, Perception, Sleight of Hand, Stealth

6th Level Feature

By 6th level

Psionic Strikes

At 9th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with psionic energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 psychic damage to the target.

When you reach 17th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

Crown of Despair

Avatar Discipline

You have learned to harvest seeds of despair in a creature's psyche, wracking it with self doubt and inaction.

Psychic Focus. While focused on this discipline, you have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks.

Crowned in Sorrow (1+ psi points). As an action, one creature you can see within 60 feet of you must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save it takes 1d8 psychic damage per psi point spent and it cannot take reactions until the start of its next turn. On a success it takes half as much damage.

Succumb to Apathy (2 psi points). As a reaction when you see a creature within 60 feet of you make a saving throw, you can impose disadvantage on that saving throw.

Visions of Despair (3+ psi points). As an action, you force one creature you can see within 60 feet of you to make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save it takes 1d6 psychic damage per psi point spent and its speed is reduced to 0 until the end of its next turn. On a success it takes half as much damage.

Embrace of Hopelessness (5 psi points; conc., 1 min). As an action, you force up to three creatures you can see within 60 feet of you to make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, those creatures are slowed, as if under the effect of the Slow spell. Those creatures can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of their turns, ending the effect on themselves on a success.

Dolorous Mind (7+ psi points; conc., 1 min). As an action, you choose one creature you can see within 60 feet of you. It must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be incapacitated and have a speed of 0 until this effect ends. The creature can repeat this saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. For each psi point above 7 you spend using this feature, you can target one additional creature within range.

Eye of Ennui (9 psi points; conc., 1 min). As an action you create an aura of despair in a 30 foot radius sphere centered on point within 120 feet you can see. Each creature that is within the area when you use this feature or start its turn there must make a Charisma saving throw or be overwhelmed by despair for the duration. During this time, the creature cannot have a speed higher than 10 feet and has disadvantage on attack rolls, ability and saving throws other than Charisma saving throws. If a creature ends its turn outside of the sphere, they can repeat this saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest.

Intellect Fortress

Awakened Discipline

You have practiced mental exercises to mystically protect your body and mind.

Psychic Focus. While focusing on this discipline, you have a +1 bonus to Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma saving throws.

Psychic Feedback (1+ psi points). As a reaction when you are dealt damage to by a creature, you deal 1d8 psychic damage to that creature for each psi point spent on this discipline.

Mystical Ward (2 psi points; conc., 10 min). As an action you raise psychic wards against spiritual threats. While this discipline is active you are protected as if you were under the effects of the Protection from Evil and Good spell.

Mental Barrier (3 psi points; 8 hours). As an action you veil your mind using psychic power. For the duration your mind cannot be read nor detected, creatures can’t telepathically communicate with you unless you allow it, and you have advantage on saving throws against any effect that would determine whether you are telling the truth.

Reinforced Psyche (5 psi points; conc., 1 hour). As an action you increase your mental fortitude. For the duration you have resistance to psychic damage, as well has advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma saving throws.

Psionic Warding (7 psi points; 8 hours). As an action you project an aura of disruptive psionic energy. For the duration you and creatures within 30 feet of you cannot be targeted by any divination magic nor magical or psionic scrying sensors.

Capstone (9 psi points). Description.

Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest.

Mantle of Joy

Avatar Discipline

You have learned to tap into your joy, radiating out an energizing psychic energy.

Psychic Focus. While focused on this discipline, you have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks.

Instill Confidence (1+ psi points). As an action, one creature you can see within 60 feet of you gains 1d4 temporary hit points for each psi point spent.

Joyous Presence (2 psi points). As a reaction when you see a creature within 60 feet of you make a saving throw, you can grant advantage on that saving throw.

Aura of Jubilation (3 psi points; conc., 1 minute). As an action choose up to 3 creatures you can see within 60 feet of you. Whenever one of those creatures makes an attack roll or ability check while the effect lasts, that creature may roll a d4 and add it to that roll. For each psi point you spend above 3, you can target one additional creature within range.

Dolorous Mind (5+ psi points; conc., 1 min). As an action, you choose one creature you can see within 60 feet of you. It must succeed on a Charisma saving thorw or be incapacitated and have a speed of 0 until this effect ends. The creature can repeat this saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. For each psi point above 5 you spend using this feature, you can target one additional creature within range.

Capstone (9 psi points). Description.

Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest.

Psychic Assault

Awakened Discipline

You have practiced using your mind itself as a weapon, unleashing powerful aggressive psionic abilities.

Psychic Focus. While focused on this discipline, you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls with psionic talents that deal psychic damage.

Psionic Spike (1+ psi points). As an action you force one creature you can see within 90 feet of you to make an Intelligence saving throw, taking 1d10 psychic damage for each psi point spent on this discipline on a failure. On a success the creature takes half as much damage.

Telekinetic Lance (2+ psi points). As an action, make a discipline attack roll against a target you can see within 90 feet of yourself. On a hit that target takes 3d8 force damage. This damage increases by 1d8 for each psi point expended on this discipline beyond 2.

Psychic Wave (3+ psi points). As an action, you force creatures within a 30 foot cone (originating from you) to make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save the target takes 3d6 psychic damage, plus an additional 2d6 psychic damage for each psi point spent on this discipline beyond 3. On a successful save, it takes half as much damage.

Overwhelm the Psyche(5 psi points). Choose a 20-foot cube within 90 feet of yourself. Each creature within that area must make an Intelligence saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn.

Psionic Blast (7+ psi points). As an action, you force each creature within 30 feet of you to make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save the target takes 1d8 force damage per psi point spent on this discpline and is pushed 30 feet directly away from you and knocked prone. On a successful save, a target takes half as much damage and isn’t pushed or knocked prone.

Psyche Ravage (9 psi points). As an action, you force one creature you can see within 120 feet of you to make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save the creature takes 7d12 psychic damage and is stunned for 1 minute. On a success a creature takes half damage and isn't stunned. A stunned creature can make an Intelligence saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Once you use this feature, you cannot use it again until you complete a long rest.

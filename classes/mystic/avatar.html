Mystic: Avatar

Mystics who dedicate themselves to the Order of the Avatar seek to master emotions. Tapping into the raw emotions that drive creatures, some seek dominion over others as manipulators and tyrants, and others seek to bring joy and purpose to the world.

Bonus Disciplines

You learn bonus disciplines associated with your order. When you select this order at 1st level and again at 10th level, choose a psionic discipline from the Order Disciplines table to learn. These disciplines do not count against the number of disciplines you can know.

Order Disciplines
Crown of Despair
Crown of Serenity
Mantle of Joy
Mantle of Rage

Bonus Proficiencies

Starting at 1st level when you choose this order, you gain proficiency with two of the following skills (your choice): Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion.

6th Level Feature

By 6th level

Potent Talents

At 9th level your focus on destructive power has strengthened your psionic talents. You can add your Intelligence modifier to the damage rolls of psionic talents you use.

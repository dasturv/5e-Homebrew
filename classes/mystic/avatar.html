Mystic: Avatar

Mystics who dedicate themselves to the Order of the Avatar seek to master emotions. Tapping into the raw emotions that drive creatures, some seek dominion over others as manipulators and tyrants, and others seek to bring joy and purpose to the world.

Bonus Disciplines

You learn bonus disciplines associated with your order. When you select this order at 1st level and again at 10th level, choose a psionic discipline from the Order Disciplines table to learn. These disciplines do not count against the number of disciplines you can know.

Order Disciplines
Crown of Despair
Crown of Serenity
Mantle of Joy
Mantle of Rage
Mantle of Terror

Bonus Proficiencies

Starting at 1st level when you choose this order, you gain proficiency with one of the following skills (your choice): Deception, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion.

Aura of Emotion

By 6th level your mastery over emotions manifests in a powerful aura around you. When you enter a trance using your psychic trance feature, you produce an aura in a 15-foot radius centered on you for the duration of the trance. Choose either energizing or enervating. You can choose a different option each time you enter a trance.

  • Energizing. Whenever you or a friendly creature ends their turn within the aura, they gain a number of temporary hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier.
  • Enervating. Whenever a hostile creature starts their turn in the aura or enters the aura for the first time on their turn, they take psychic damage equal to your Intelligence modifier.

Potent Talents

At 9th level your focus on destructive power has strengthened your psionic talents. You can add your Intelligence modifier to the damage rolls of psionic talents you use.

Improved Aura

By 14th level the potency of your aura has reached its peak. The radius of your aura of emotion increases to 30 feet. In addition your aura gains an additional effect based on if your chose an energizing or enervating aura:

  • Energizing. Whenever you or friendly creatures within the aura would recover hit points, they recover bonus hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier.
  • Enervating. Hostile creatures within the aura cannot recover hit points nor gain temporary hit points.

Aerial Eidolon

Small and flight capable, these Eidolons deftly weave through the battlefield.

Aerial Eidolon
Small aberration, celestial, elemental, fey, fiend or undead (your choice), same alignment as you
Armor Class: 14 (natural armor)
Hit Points: 4 + four times your summoner level (the eidolon has a number of Hit Dice [d6s] equal to your summoner level)
Speed: 20 feet, fly 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
9 (-1) 16 (+3) 10 (+0) 13 (+1) 15 (+2) 12 (+1)
Saving Throws: Dex +(3+PB), Wis +(2+PB)
Skills: Acrobatics +(3+PB), Perception +(2+PB)
Senses: darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12 + PB
Languages: all languages you know
Challenge: -
Magical Nature: The Eidolon does not need to eat, drink, breath or sleep. Additionally, its attacks are considered magical for the purposes of overcoming damage resistances.
Flyby: The Eidolon doesn't provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy's reach.
Actions
Shred. Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d4 + 3 + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack).

Aquatic Eidolon

Amphibious and strong, these Eidolons help control the battlefield on both land and water.

Aquatic Eidolon
Medium aberration, celestial, elemental, fey, fiend or undead (your choice), same alignment as you
Armor Class: 15 (natural armor)
Hit Points: 6 + six times your summoner level (the eidolon has a number of Hit Dice [d8s] equal to your summoner level)
Speed: 30 feet, swim 30 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
16 (+3) 15 (+2) 12 (+1) 13 (+1) 10 (+0) 9 (-1)
Saving Throws: Str +(3+PB), Dex +(2+PB)
Skills: Athletics +(3+PB), Stealth +(2+PB)
Senses: darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
Languages: all languages you know
Challenge: -
Magical Nature: The Eidolon does not need to eat, drink, breath or sleep. Additionally, its attacks are considered magical for the purposes of overcoming damage resistances.
Slippery: The Eidolon has advantage on ability checks and saving throws to escape a grapple.
Actions
Binding Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6 + 3 + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack), and the target is grappled (escape DC equals your spell save DC). Until this grapple ends the Eidolon can't use this attack on another target.

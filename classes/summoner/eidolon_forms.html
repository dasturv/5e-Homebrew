Hulking Eidolon

Large, strong and tough, these Eidolons thrive in the thick of battle.

Hulking Eidolon
Medium aberration, celestial, fey, fiend or undead (your choice), same alignment as you
Armor Class: 17 (natural armor)
Hit Points: 7 + seven times your summoner level (the eidolon has a number of Hit Dice [d8s] equal to your summoner level)
Speed: 30 feet
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
16 (+3) 9 (-1) 15 (+2) 10 (+0) 13 (+1) 12 (+1)
Saving Throws: Str +(3+PB), Con +(2+PB)
Skills: Athletics +(3+PB), Intimidation +(1+PB)
Senses: darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
Languages: all languages you know
Challenge: -
Strange Nature: the Eidolon does not need to eat, drink, breath or sleep.
Battle Armor: critical hits on the Eidolon are treated as normal hits instead.
Actions
Rend. Melee Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d8 + 3 + PB slashing damage.

Summoner: Domineering

Summoners who form a domineering relationship with their Eidolon enforce their will with an iron fist. Perhaps you see your Eidolon as little more than a tool, or perhaps you Eidolon is a violent or dangerous spirit that respects only strength.

Bonus Evolution

When you choose this relationship at 3rd level, your Eidolon gains the Fearsome I evolution. This evolution does not count against the number of evolutions your Eidolon can have.

Vented Frustration

At 6th level your Eidolon's frustrations manifest in violent outbursts. Whenever it takes damage from a creature within its reach, it can use its reaction to make a melee attack against that creature. The Eidolon can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Terrible Rule

Upon reaching 9th level, your Eidolon only fears you. It has advantage on saving throws to end or prevent being charmed or frightened by any creature other than you.

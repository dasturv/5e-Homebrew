Summoner: Friendly

Summoners who form a friendly relationship with their Eidolon have a constant companion. Such pairings are often sources of joy and mirth wherever they go. Perhaps you befriended your Eidolon as a child, or maybe you both bonded over a similar sense of humor.

Relationship Spells

Bonus Evolution

When you choose this relationship at 3rd level, your Eidolon gains the Restorative Touch I evolution. This evolution does not count against the number of evolutions your Eidolon can have.

Helping Hand

At 6th level you and your Eidolon have learned to work in sync. Whenever a creature within 5 feet of your Eidolon makes a saving throw against a spell you cast, it can use its reaction to impose disadvantage on that saving throw. The Eidolon can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Quality Time

Upon reaching 9th level you bond with your Eidolon makes it easier for both of you to relax. If you or your Eidolon are within 30 feet of each other and regain hit points at the end of a short rest by spending one or more Hit Dice, each of you regains an extra 1d8 hit points.

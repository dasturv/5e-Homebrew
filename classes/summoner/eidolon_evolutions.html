Agile I

Your Eidolon becomes quicker. Its movement speeds increase by 5 feet.

Agile II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Agile I
Your Eidolon becomes even faster. Its movement speeds increase by another 5 feet, for a total bonus of 10 feet.

Ambusher I

Your Eidolon has improved reflexes. It gains a bonus to its initiative rolls equal to you proficiency bonus.

Ambusher II

Prerequisite: 11th level, Ambusher I or the Quick Reflexes trait
Your Eidolon's becomes a perfect ambush predator. If it hits a creature with a weapon attack roll, the creature takes an extra 2d6 damage if it hasnâ€™t taken a turn yet in the current combat.

Camouflaged I

Your Eidolon's skin or hide blends in with its environment. It has advantage on Dexterity (stealth) checks.

Camouflaged II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Camouflaged I
Your Eidolon's stealth capabilities have advanced to the point of invisibility. It can use a bonus action to become invisible until the end of its next turn or until it attacks, makes a damage roll or forces someone to make a saving throw. The Eidolon can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Climber I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to scale sheer surfaces. It gains a climb speed of 30 feet.

Climber II

Prerequisite: Climber I or an innate climb speed
Your Eidolon's climbing abilities improve. It can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

Consume

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Ensnaring Strike I
Your Eidolon gains the ability to swallow its foes whole. When it hits a creature it has grappled and is at least one size smaller than itself with its Ensnaring Strike attack, it can swallow that creature, ending the grapple. A swallowed creature is blinded and restrained, has total cover against attacks and other effects outside of the Eidolon, and takes 2d4 acid damage at the start of each of your turns. The Eidolon can only have one target swallowed at a time.

If the Eidolon is banished, dismissed or is reduced to 0 hit points, the creature is deposited in the Eidolon's previous space and is prone.

Debilitating Strike I

Your Eidolon uses claws, spines or other such appendages to hurt and distract its foes. It gains the following attack:

Debilitating Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6 + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack) and the target has disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

Debilitating Strike II

Prerequisite: 8th Level, Debilitating Strike I
Your Eidolon's ability to harm and distract improves. A creature hit by its Debilitating Strike attack also has its speed halved (rounded down) until the end of its next turn.

Defensive I

Your Eidolon's defenses improve. It gains a +2 bonus to AC.

Defensive II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Defensive I
Your Eidolon's defenses improve even further. It gains a further +2 bonus to AC, for a total of +4.

Distracting I

Your Eidolon is capable of momentarily distracting your foes, leaving them open to your magics. Whenever a creature within the Eidolon's reach makes a saving throw against one of your spells, it can use its reaction to impose disadvantage on that saving throw. The Eidolon can use this reaction a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Distracting II

Prerequisite: 14th level, Distracting I
Your Eidolon's distracting abilities improve. Creatures within 5 feet of the Eidolon have disadvantage on attack rolls on creatures other than the Eidolon or creatures with this feature.

Energy Affinity I

Your Eidolon becomes attuned with a specific energy. Choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Necrotic, Poison, Psychic, Radiant or Thunder. It gains resistance to the chosen damage type.

Energy Affinity II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Energy Affinity I or resistance to the chosen damage type
Your Eidolon's energetic powers increase. Its weapon attacks deal an additional 1d4 damage of the chosen type.

Energy Affinity III

Prerequisite: 17th Level, Energy Affinity II
Your Eidolon's energetic powers reach their apex. It gains immunity to the chosen damage type, and its bonus damage increases to 2d4.

Elemental Body I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon begins to radiate elemental energy. Choose one of the following damage types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison or Thunder. Whenever a creature within 5 feet of the Eidolon touches it or hits it with a melee attack, they take 1d4 damage of the chosen type.

Elemental Body II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Elemental Body I or the Energy Aura feature
Your Eidolon's aura of elemental power increases in potency. The damage of its Elemental Body evolution increases to 2d4. Additionially, at the start of the Eidolon's turn, each hostile creature within 5 feet of it takes 2d4 damage of the chosen type.

Elemental Burst I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to produce a breath weapon of elemental energy. Choose one of the following damage types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison or Thunder. The Eidolon gains the following action:

Elemental Burst. The Eidolon exhales elemental energy in a 15-foot cone. Each creature within that area must make a Dexterity (if your chose acid, fire or lightning) or Constitution (if you chose cold, poison or thunder) saving throw. On a failed save a target takes 3d6 damage of the chosen damage type. On a success it takes half as much damage instead. The Eidolon can use this action a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Elemental Burst II

Prerequisite: 8th Level, Elemental Burst I
Your Eidolon's breath weapon grows stronger. Its Elemental Burst's damage increases to 5d6, and its area increases to a 30-foot cone.

Elemental Burst III

Prerequisite: 17th Level, Elemental Burst II
Your Eidolon's breath weapon has reached its peak. Its Elemental Burst's damage increases to 7d6, and its area increases to a 60-foot cone.

Ensnaring Strike I

Your Eidolon uses claws, tentacles or other such appendages to bind its foes. It gains the following attack:

Ensnaring Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6 + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack), and the target is grappled (escape DC equals your spell save DC). Until this grapple ends the Eidolon can't use this attack on another target.

Ensnaring Strike II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Ensnaring Strike I or the Binding Strike attack
Your Eidolon's binding capabilities improve. Its Ensnaring Strike attack has its reach increased by 5 feet, and it can have up to three creatures grappled at a time.

Esoteric Burst I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to produce a bolt of strange energies. Choose one of the following damage types: Necrotic, Psychic or Radiant. The Eidolon gains the following action:

Esoteric Burst. The Eidolon makes a spell attack roll against a target it can see within 90 feet of itself, using your spell attack modifier. On a hit the target takes 3d8 damage of the chosen damage type. The Eidolon can use this action a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Esoteric Burst II

Prerequisite: 8th Level, Esoteric Burst I
Your Eidolon's strange powers grow stronger. Its Esoteric Burst's damage increases to 5d8, and its range increases to 120 feet.

Esoteric Burst III

Prerequisite: 17th Level, Esoteric Burst II
Your Eidolon's esoteric powers have reached their peak. Its Esoteric Burst's damage increases to 7d8, and its range increases to 300 feet.

Evasive I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon becomes harder to pin down in a fight. It can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.

Evasive II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Evasive I or the Nimble Escape feature
Your Eidolon's evasiveness increases further. When it is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.

Extrasensory I

Your Eidolon's perceptions go beyond normal means. It can sense the presence of magic within 30 feet of it, as if it was under the effects of the Detect Magic spell.

Extrasensory II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Extrasensory I
Your Eidolon's perceptions increase even further. It gains truesight to a range of 60 feet.

Fearsome I

Your Eidolon is a terrifying force. The Eidolon gains the following bonus action:

Terrifying Presence. Creatures of the Eidolon's choice within 15 feet of it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or become frightened of the Eidolon until the end of your next turn. Once the Eidolon uses this feature, it cannot use it again until you complete a short or long rest.

Fearsome II

Prerequisite: 14th level, Fearsome I
Your Eidolon's terrifying presence improves. The area of its Terrifying Presence feature increases to 30 feet. Additionally, when a creature fails its saving throw, it becomes frightened of the Eidolon for 1 minute. A frightened creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. Once a creature succeeds on this saving throw, it is immune to the Eidolon's Terrifying Presence for 24 hours.

Flexible I

Your Eidolon's body is maleable and flexible. It has advantage on ability checks and saving throws to escape a grapple.

Flexible II

Prerequisite: Flexible I or the Slippery trait
Your Eidolon's form is completely amorphous. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

Flight I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon sprouts wings or magically begins to levitate. It gains a fly speed of 30 feet.

Flight II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Flight I or an innate fly speed
Your Eidolon's aerial abilities improve. Its fly speed is increased to 60 feet.

Helpful I

Your Eidolon is incredibly helpful to you. The Eidolon can take the help action as a bonus action. The Eidolon can use this bonus action a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Helpful II

Prerequisite: 5th level, Helpful I
Your Eidolon's aid improves. Whenever it takes the help action as a bonus action using its Helpful evolution, it can choose one of the following additional benefits:

  • Both the Eidolon and the helped creature gain temporary hit points equal to your proficiency bonus.
  • Both the Eidolon and the helped creature's movement speed are increased by 5 feet and opportunity attacks against them have disadvantage until the start of your next turn.
  • Both the Eidolon and the helped creature deal 1d4 bonus damage on their next damage roll they make before the start of your next turn.

Interception I

Your Eidolon has learned to block and parry attacks in order to defend its allies. Whenever a creature within the Eidolon's reach is hit by an attack, it can use its reaction to reduce that damage by 1d10 plus your proficiency bonus, to a minimum of 0 damage.

Interception II

Prerequisite: 11th level, Interception I
Your Eidolon's protective capabilities improve. It may use one additional reaction per round. This bonus reaction can only be used to reduce damage using its Interception I evolution or make opportunity attacks. Only one reaction can be used per trigger.

Magical Resistance I

Prerequisite: 11th Level
Your Eidolon becomes resistant to magic. It has advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

Magical Resistance II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Magical Resistance I
Your Eidolon's magical protections increase in potency. It gains resistance to damage from spells.

Hardened Carapace I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon's hide or shell hardens. It treats critcial hits against itself as normal hits.

Hardened Carapace II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Hardened Carapace I or the Battle Armor feature
Your Eidolon's armor hardens even further. It has resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing and slashing damage.

Massive Growth I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon swells in size. Both its size and hit dice increase by one size. If your Eidolon is large or larger, you can use it as a mount.

Massive Growth II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Massive Growth I
Your Eidolon further grows. Both its size and hit dice increase by one size. In addition, its reach is increased by 5 feet.

Projectile I

Your Eidolon launches spines, bolts or other such projectiles. It gains the following attack:

Projectile. Ranged Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, range 60/180 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6 + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack).

Projectile II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Projectile I or the Bolt attack
Your Eidolon's ranged capabilities improve. Its Projectile attack has its range increases to 150/450 and ignores half and three-quarters cover.

Resilient I

Your Eidolon becomes hardier. Its hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to your level. Whenever you gain a level thereafter, its hit point maximum increases by an additional 1 hit point.

Resilient II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Resilient I
Your Eidolon becomes even hardier. Its hit point maximum increases by an amount equal to your level (for a total of twice you level). Whenever you gain a level thereafter, its hit point maximum increases by an additional 1 hit point (for a total of 2).

Restorative Touch I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to heal at a touch. The Eidolon gains the following action:

Restorative Touch. The Eidolon touches a creature within its reach. The target recovers 2d4 + PB hit points. The Eidolon can use this action a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Restorative Touch II

Prerequisite: 8th Level, Restorative Touch I
Your Eidolon's healing powers grow stronger. The amount of hit points recovered by its Restorative Touch feature increases to 4d4 + PB.

Restorative Touch III

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Restorative Touch II
Your Eidolon's healing powers have reached their peak. The amount of hit points recovered by its Restorative Touch feature increases to 6d4 + PB.

Savagery I

Your Eidolon's natural weapons improve. The damage die for its weapon attacks increase by one size.

Savagery II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Savagery I
Your Eidolon's weapons improve even further. The damage die for its weapon attacks increase by a further one size. Additionally, it scores a critical hit on a 19 or 20 when using its weapon attacks.

Swimmer I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to swim with grace. It gains a swim speed of 30 feet. Additionally, it can breathe both water and air.

Swimmer II

Prerequisite: 11th level, Swimmer I or an innate swim speed
Your Eidolon's swimming abilities improve. Its swim speed is increased to 60 feet.

Violent I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon's bloodlust grows. It can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature it can see as a bonus action on its turn.

Violent II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Violent I or the Aggressive feature
Your Eidolon's violent tendencies reach their peak. It has advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all of its hit points.

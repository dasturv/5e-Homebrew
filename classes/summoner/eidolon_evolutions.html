Agile I

Your Eidolon becomes quicker. Its movement speeds increase by 5 feet.

Agile II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Agile I
Your Eidolon becomes even faster. Its movement speeds increase by another 5 feet, for a total bonus of 10 feet.

Ambusher I

Your Eidolon has improved reflexes. It gains a bonus to its initiative rolls equal to you proficiency bonus.

Ambusher II

Prerequisite: 11th level, Ambusher I or the Quick Reflexes trait
Your Eidolon's becomes a perfect ambush predator. If it hits a creature with a weapon attack roll, the creature takes an extra 2d6 damage if it hasnâ€™t taken a turn yet in the current combat.

Camouflaged I

Your Eidolon's skin or hide blends in with its environment. It has advantage on Dexterity (stealth) checks.

Camouflaged II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Camouflaged I
Your Eidolon's stealth capabilities have advanced to the point of invisibility. It can use a bonus action to become invisible until the end of its next turn or until it attacks, makes a damage roll or forces someone to make a saving throw. The Eidolon can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Climber I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to scale sheer surfaces. It gains a climb speed of 30 feet.

Climber II

Prerequisite: Climber I or an innate climb speed
Your Eidolon's climbing abilities improve. It can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

Defensive I

Your Eidolon's defenses improve. It gains a +2 bonus to AC.

Defensive II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Defensive I
Your Eidolon's defenses improve even further. It gains a further +2 bonus to AC, for a total of +4.

Energy Affinity I

Your Eidolon becomes attuned with a specific energy. Choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Necrotic, Poison, Psychic, Radiant or Thunder. It gains resistance to the chosen damage type.

Energy Affinity II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Energy Affinity I
Your Eidolon's energetic powers increase. Its weapon attacks deal an additional 1d4 damage of the chosen type.

Energy Affinity III

Prerequisite: 17th Level, Energy Affinity II
Your Eidolon's energetic powers reach their apex. It gains immunity to the chosen damage type, and its bonus damage increases to 2d4.

Elemental Burst I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to produce a breath weapon of elemental energy. Choose one of the following damage types: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison or Thunder. The Eidolon gains the following action:

Elemental Burst. The Eidolon exhales elemental energy in a 15-foot cone. Each creature within that area must make a Dexterity (if your chose acid, fire or lightning) or Constitution (if you chose cold, poison or thunder) saving throw. On a failed save a target takes 3d6 damage of the chosen damage type. On a success it takes half as much damage instead. The Eidolon can use this action a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Elemental Burst II

Prerequisite: 8th Level, Elemental Burst I
Your Eidolon's breath weapon grows stronger. Its Elemental Burst's damage increases to 5d6, and its area increases to a 30-foot cone.

Elemental Burst III

Prerequisite: 17th Level, Elemental Burst II
Your Eidolon's breath weapon has reached its peak. Its Elemental Burst's damage increases to 7d6, and its area increases to a 60-foot cone.

Ensnaring Strike I

Your Eidolon uses claws, tentacles or other such appendages to bind its foes. It gains the following attack:

Ensnaring Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6 + Str + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack), and the target is grappled (escape DC equals your spell save DC). Until this grapple ends the Eidolon can't use this attack on another target.

Ensnaring Strike II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Ensnaring Strike I or the Binding Strike attack
Your Eidolon's binding capabilities improve. Its Ensnaring Strike attack has its reach increased by 5 feet, and it can have up to three creatures grappled at a time.

Esoteric Burst I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to produce a bolt of strange energies. Choose one of the following damage types: Necrotic, Psychic or Radiant. The Eidolon gains the following action:

Esoteric Burst. The Eidolon makes a spell attack roll against a target it can see within 90 feet of itself, using your spell attack modifier. On a hit the target takes 3d8 damage of the chosen damage type. The Eidolon can use this action a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regains all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Esoteric Burst II

Prerequisite: 8th Level, Esoteric Burst I
Your Eidolon's strange powers grow stronger. Its Esoteric Burst's damage increases to 5d8, and its range increases to 120 feet.

Esoteric Burst III

Prerequisite: 17th Level, Esoteric Burst II
Your Eidolon's esoteric powers have reached their peak. Its Esoteric Burst's damage increases to 7d8, and its range increases to 300 feet.

Evasive I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon becomes harder to pin down in a fight. It can take the Dash, Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.

Evasive II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Evasive I or the Cunning Action feature
Your Eidolon's evasiveness increases further. When it is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.

Flexible I

Your Eidolon's body is maleable and flexible. It has advantage on ability checks and saving throws to escape a grapple.

Flexible II

Prerequisite: Flexible I or the Slippery trait
Your Eidolon's form is completely amorphous. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

Flight I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon sprouts wings or magically begins to levitate. It gains a fly speed of 30 feet.

Flight II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Flight I or an innate fly speed
Your Eidolon's aerial abilities improve. Its fly speed is increased to 60 feet.

Hardened Carapace I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon's hide or shell hardens. It treats critcial hits against itself as normal hits.

Hardened Carapace II

Prerequisite: 14th Level, Hardened Carapace I or the Battle Armor feature
Your Eidolon's armor hardens even further. It has resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing and slashing damage.

Massive Growth I

Prerequisite: 5th Level
Your Eidolon swells in size. Both its size and hit dice increase by one size. If your Eidolon is large or larger, you can use it as a mount.

Massive Growth II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Massive Growth I
Your Eidolon further grows. Both its size and hit dice increase by one size. In addition, its reach is increased by 5 feet.

Projectile I

Your Eidolon launches spines, bolts or other such projectiles. It gains the following attack:

Projectile. Ranged Weapon Attack: your spell attack modifier to hit, range 60/180 ft., one target. Hit: 1d6 + Dex + PB bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose when you gain this attack).

Projectile II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Projectile I or the Bolt attack
Your Eidolon's ranged capabilities improve. Its Projectile attack has its range increases to 150/450 and ignores half and three-quarters cover.

Savagery I

Your Eidolon's natural weapons improve. The damage die for its weapon attacks increase by one size.

Savagery II

Prerequisite: 11th Level, Savagery I
Your Eidolon's weapons improve even further. The damage die for its weapon attacks increase by a further one size. Additionally, it scores a critical hit on a 19 or 20 when using its weapon attacks.

Swimmer I

Your Eidolon gains the ability to swim with grace. It gains a swim speed of 30 feet. Additionally, it can breathe both water and air.

Swimmer II

Prerequisite: 11th level, Swimmer I or an innate swim speed
Your Eidolon's swimming abilities improve. Its swim speed is increased to 60 feet.

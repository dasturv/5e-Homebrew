Summoner: Protective

Summoners who form a protective relationship with their Eidolon have a fiercely loyal guardian. Perhaps your Eidolon had watched over you throughout your life, or perhaps you saved it in some way and now it seeks to repay its debt to you.

Relationship Spells
Summoner Level Spells
1st Protection from Evil and Good, Blade Ward
5th Reflexive Barrier
9th Remove Curse
13th Death Ward
17th Antilife Shell

Bonus Evolution

When you choose this relationship at 3rd level, your Eidolon gains the Interception I evolution. This evolution does not count against the number of evolutions your Eidolon can have.

Stalwart Defender

At 6th level you have learned how to best utilize your Eidolon's protection. You are considered to have half cover while within 5 feet of your Eidolon, provided neither you nor it are incapacitated.

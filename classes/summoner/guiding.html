Summoner: Guiding

Summoners who form a guiding relationship with their Eidolon see them as a mentor or spiritual guide. Perhaps your Eidolon reached out to you with answers at a dark point in your life, or perhaps you come from a culture who treasure and worships spirits.

Bonus Evolution

When you choose this relationship at 3rd level, your Eidolon gains the Evolution evolution. This evolution does not count against the number of evolutions your Eidolon can have.

Architect: Arcane Geometer

An Arcane Geometer is one who studies the fundamental geometries of reality. One part studies, one part spiritual journey, an Arcane Geometer finds endless beauty in their work. Able to call forth constructs made of solidified arcane light, they cover the battlefield in scintillating brilliance.

Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this framework at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with jeweler's tools. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan's tools of your choice.

Additionally, your proficiency bonus is now doubled for any ability check you make that uses your proficiency with jeweler's tools.

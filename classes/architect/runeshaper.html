Architect: Runeshaper

The Runeshaper controls the magic of runes. Originally a practice developed by Giants, the Dwarves and underdark races have since brought runeshaping to the wider world. Creating constructs infused with elemental power, a Runeshaper is a primal force.

Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this framework at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with mason's tools. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan's tools of your choice.

Additionally, your proficiency bonus is now doubled for any ability check you make that uses your proficiency with a mason's tools.

Runic Infusion

Also at 3rd level, you gain the ability infuse yourself and your constructions with elemental power. Whenever you complete a long rest, choose cold, fire, lightning or thunder. You gain resistance to the chosen damage type until you complete another long rest. Additionally, whenever one of your constructions would deal force damage, you may have it deal the chosen damage type instead.

Sturdy Constructions

Beginning at 6th level, your constructions become harder to destroy. Constructions you create have 10 additional hit points.

Runic Scorching

Starting at 10th level your elemental powers increase. When you or one of your constructions are dealt damage by a creature you can see within 60 feet of you, you may use your reaction to force that creature to make a Dexterity saving throw against your construction save DC. The creature takes 3d6 damage of the type chosen with your Runic Infusion feature, or half as much on a success.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier, and regain all expended uses when you complete a long rest.

Elemental Mastery

By 15th level, your control over your runes has reached new heights. As an action you can change the damage type chosen with your Runic Infusion feature with another option from that feature. Additionally, the damage dealt by your Runic Scorching feature increases to 4d6.

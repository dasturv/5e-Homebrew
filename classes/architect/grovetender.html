Architect: Grovetender

An ancient tradition tied to druids and those who seek to protect nature, the Grovetender uses constructs of living leaf and wood to safeguard sacred sites. Trying to shake such an Architect from their grove is a difficult task, as the ground itself turns against intruders.

Bonus Proficiencies

When you choose this framework at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with herbalist's supplies. If you already have this proficiency, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan's tools of your choice.

Additionally, your proficiency bonus is now doubled for any ability check you make that uses your proficiency with a herbalist's supplies.

Heart of the Grove

Also at 3rd level, you gain the ability to create a magical grove. You learn a special construction: the Groveheart. The groveheart does not count against the maximum number of constructions you can know. When creating your groveheart you create it in an unoccupied space you can see within 15 feet of you. The groveheart cannot have a blueprint applied to it, and you can have only one groveheart active at a time.

Your groveheart creates your grove in a 15-foot radius sphere centered on itself. Spaces within your grove are considered to be difficult terrain to creatures hostile to you.

Living Constructions

Beginning at 6th level, your constructions living nature makes the mincredibly resilient within your grove. Whenever one of your constructions would be destroyed within your grove, you may roll a D20 (no action required). On a 10 or higher, it is instead reduced to 1 hit point. The minimum number you need to roll increases by 1 for each time this feature successfully prevents a construction from being destroyed, and is reset to 10 whenever you complete a long rest.

Sacred Ground

Starting at 10th level, your grove's hatred towards intruders takes a more potent form. Whenever a creature hostile to you ends its turn within your grove, it takes 1d4 force damage.

Creeping Growth

By 15th level, your constructions can extend your grove. Each of your constructions within your grove expand it in a 10-foot sphere centered around itself, provided you can draw a path between it and your groveheart without leaving your grove.

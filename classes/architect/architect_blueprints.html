Attracting

The target is pulled 10 feet towards the construction.

Disrupting

The next time the target makes saving throw before the start of its next turn, it rolls a d4 and subtracts it from the total.

Distracting

The next attack roll the target makes before the end of its next turn is made at disadvantage.

Fortifying

You may have the target gain 1d4 + your intelligence modifier temporary hit points. Any of these temporary hit points remaining at the start of the targets next turn are removed.

Honing

The next time the target makes an attack roll before the start of its next turn, you may have it roll a d4 and add it to the total.

Invigorating

You may increase the target's movement speed by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

Leeching

The target takes 1d4 force damage and cannot recover hit points until the end of its next turn.

Marking

The next attack roll made against the target before the start of its next turn is made at advantage.

Painful

The target takes 1d6 force damage.

Repelling

The target is pushed 10 feet away from the construction.

Stunning

The target takes 1d4 force damage and cannot take reactions until the end of its next turn.

Suppressing

The target has its movement speed halved until the end of its next turn.

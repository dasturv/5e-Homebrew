Zal Ighur, the Relentless

In a world so urban, so civilized, the ancient white dragon Zal offers a different route. Living deep in harsh wild space, she prowls as an apex predator, hunting beasts and sentients alike who trespass upon her territory. Despite her animalistic leanings, she still maintains a foothold in Valastar's wider world through her leaderhsip of the hunter's guild, an organization made up of Valastar's greatest bounty hunters and monster slayers. She leads her followers as a pack, rewarding the strong and hunting the weak.

Actions
Multiattack.Zal can use her Frightful Presence. She then makes three attacks: one with her bite and two with her claws.
Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (2d10 + 9) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) cold damage.
Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d6 + 9) slashing damage.
Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d8 + 9) bludgeoning damage.
Wing Talons. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d8 + 9) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) cold damage. The target must succeed on a DC 24 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
Frightful Presence. Each creature of Zal's choice that is within 120 feet of her and aware of her must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to Zal's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.
Cold Breath (Recharge 5-6). Zal exhales an icy blast in a 90-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 23 Dexterity saving throw, taking 72 (16d8) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
Legendary Actions

Zal can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

Detect. Zal makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.
Tail Attack. Zal makes a tail attack.
Wing Talon Attack (Costs 2 actions). Zal makes a wing talon attack. She then can move up to half her speed.
Cast Spell (Costs 2 actions). Zal casts one spell. She can only take this action if she has not cast a spell since the start of her last turn.

Voidworm

The least of the proteans (to the point where greater proteans see them as lesser cousins instead of true kin), the colorful voidworm gather in great schools within the plane of limbo. A voidworm is a serpentine creature no longer than a man's arm and covered in dazzlingly colorful scales that constantly shift hues. Curious and easily distracted, voidworms are usually peaceful creatures within their chaotic home, though they can become aggressive if they feel threatened or abused.

Voidworms as Familiars. Voidworms are popular familiars, especially with younger and more adventurous spellcasters. Their curious and whimsical nature being a source of entertainment and joy for many such spellcasters. Of course, their lack of focus and fickle attitudes give pause to older and more mature spellcasters, who may prefer a more consistent familiar. Voidworms are not particularly loyal, but will happily serve a master who travels often or seeks out new experiences. A spellcaster who settles down or abuses their familiar may quickly lose their voidworm.

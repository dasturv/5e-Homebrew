Muzcans

The Muzcans are a living horde, born of the fell god Maglataz. They live short, cruel lives under the heel of their greater kin. Their harsh treatment leads most to inflicting cruelty on others, always seeking new slaves and beasts to abuse and sacrifice to their diety. Doing so is not nearly an outlet for them, as they know that failure to provide ample sacrifices means they will be the next to be thrown into the maw of their god.

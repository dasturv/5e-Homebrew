Blazing Tyrannosaurus Rex
Huge beast, unaligned
Armor Class: 13 (natural armor)
Hit Points: 217 (15d20 + 60)
Speed: 50 ft.
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
25 (+7) 10 (+0) 19 (+4) 2 (-4) 12 (+1) 9 (-1)
Saving Throws: Str +12
Skills: Perception +6
Damage Immunities: fire
Senses: passive Perception 16
Languages: -
Challenge: 13 (10,000 XP)
Aggressive: As a bonus action, the Blazing Tyrannosaurus Rex can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.
Blazing Attacks: The Blazing Tyrannosaurus Rex's natural attacks are magical. When the Blazing Tyrannosaurus Rex hits with a natural weapon, the weapon deals an extra 7 (2d6) fire damage (included in the attack).
Fire Aura: At the start of the Blazing Tyrannosaurus Rex's turn, each creature within 10 feet of it takes 7 (2d6) fire damage, and flammable objects in the aura that aren't being worn or carried ignite.
Actions
Multiattack.The tyrannosaurus makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its tail. It can't make both attacks against the same target.
Bite. Melee Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 46 (6d12 + 7) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 19). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the tyrannosaurus can't bite another target.
Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 35 (5d10 + 7) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage.

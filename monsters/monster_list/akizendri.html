Akizendri

The bane of scholars across the planes, the Akizendri gorges itself on knowledge and written word wherever it can. The Akizendri are small serpentine creatures with a crocodile like head, humanoid arms and scales covered with constantly shifting runes and letters of countless languages.

Akizendri
Small aberration (protean), chaotic neutral
Armor Class: 12
Hit Points: 31 (7d6+7)
Speed: 30 ft., 30 ft. fly
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
9 (mod) 15 (mod) 13 (mod) 9 (mod) 11 (mod) 17 (mod)
Saving Throws: Cha +5
Damage Resistances: acid, lightning, thunder
Condition Immunities: prone
Senses: darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
Languages: Protean
Challenge: 1 (200 XP)
Immutable Form: The protean is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.
Freedom of Movement: The protean is constantly under the effects of the Freedom of Movement spell.
Actions
Information Overload. the Akizendri chooses one creature it can see within 60 feet of it. That creature must make a DC 13 Intelligence saving throw or take 10 (3d6) psychic damage. This damage is increased by 1d6 if the Akizendri has used its Consume Knowledge ability within the past minute.
Consume Knowledge (Recharge 6). The Akizendri feeds off of the knowledge from a non-magical source of text it can see within 5 feet of it, such as a book, a sign or other such object. The Akizendri regains 7 (3d4) hit points and the text is distorted. Text distorted this way is missing letters, is scrambled and has random words mixed in. Gleaning any meaning from distorted text requires a DC 25 Intelligence(investigation) check. An Akizendri cannot feed on distorted text.

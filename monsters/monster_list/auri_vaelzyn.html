Auri Vaelzyn, the Gilded Lady

The Gilded Lady is perhaps the individual with the greatest foresight of anyone in all of Valastar. Gifted with the ability to see the future, the ancient gold dragon Auri seeks to carefully guide the mortals of Valastar to a better tomorrow. Serving as an advisor to mortal politicians and founder of the Gilded Council, she patiently steers the matters of state towards an idealized future that only she can comprehend. Her advice is greatly sought after, though she usually prefers to listen rather than speak if she can. Being within her presence and listening to her wisdom is one of the most majestic and awe-inspiring experiences a mortal can have in Valastar.

Actions
Multiattack.Auri can use her Frightful Presence. She then makes three attacks: one with her bite and two with her claws.
Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +18 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (2d10 + 10) piercing damage.
Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +18 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d6 + 10) slashing damage.
Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +18 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (2d8 + 10) bludgeoning damage.
Frightful Presence. Each creature of Auri's choice that is within 120 feet of her and aware of her must succeed on a DC 25 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to Auri's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.
Breath Weapons (Recharge 5–6). Auri uses one of the following breath weapons.
Fire Breath. Auri exhales fire in a 90-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 24 Dexterity saving throw, taking 71 (13d10) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
Weakening Breath. Auri exhales gas in a 90-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 24 Strength saving throw or have disadvantage on Strength-based attack rolls, Strength checks, and Strength saving throws for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
Change Shape. Auri magically polymorphs into a humanoid or beast that has a challenge rating no higher than her own, or back into her true form. She reverts to her true form if she dies. Any equipment she is wearing or carrying is absorbed or borne by the new form (her choice).
In a new form, Auri retains her alignment, hit points, Hit Dice, ability to speak, proficiencies, Legendary Resistance, lair actions, and Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores, as well as this action. Her statistics and capabilities are otherwise replaced by those of the new form, except any class features or legendary actions of that form.
Legendary Actions

Auri can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Detect. Auri makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.
Tail Attack. Auri makes a tail attack.
Wing Attack (Costs 2 actions). Auri beats her wings. Each creature within 15 feet of she must succeed on a DC 26 Dexterity saving throw or take 17 (2d6 + 10) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. Auri can then fly up to half her flying speed.
Cast Spell (Costs 2 actions). Auri casts one spell. She can only take this action if she has not cast a spell since the start of her last turn.

Auri Vaelzyn's Lair

The Aerie Eternus is the meeting place of the Gilded Council, a coalition of ancient metallic dragons that seek to influence the politics of Valastar, with the upper levels also serving as Auri's personal lair. Unreachable without flight and vigilantly guarded by loyal draconic followers, Auri spends days at a time meditating in her gardens and libraries, seeking the best path of action to achieve a brighter future.

Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Auri takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; she can't use the same effect two rounds in a row.

  • Auri glimpses the future, so dhe has advantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws until initiative count 20 on the next round.
  • One creature Auri can see within 120 feet of herself must succeed on a DC 16 Charisma saving throw or be banished to a dream plane, a different plane of existence Auri has imagined into being. To escape, the creature must use its action to make a Charisma check contested by Auri’s. If the creature wins, it escapes the dream plane. Otherwise, the effect ends on initiative count 20 on the next round. When the effect ends, the creature reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that one is occupied.
Regional Effects

The region around Auri's lair is warped by her presence, creating the following effects:

  • Whenever a creature that can understand a language sleeps or enters a state of trance or reverie within 6 miles of Auri’s lair, she can establish telepathic contact with that creature and converse with it in its dreams. The creature remembers its conversation with her upon waking.
  • Auri is aware of all creatures within 6 miles of the lair, and can cast Scrying on them without requiring a saving throw from the target.
  • Locations and structures within 6 miles of the lair take on a dream-like, idealized form, always looking polished, vibrant and pristine.

If Auri dies, these effects end immediately.

Ruby Rod

Rod, rarity varies (requires attunement by a spellcaster)

Made of rigid black steel or iron an often stylized with ridges or spikes, this rod is capped with a large ruby. Made to resemble Asmodeus's own Ruby Rod, these rods are symbols of both rank and allegiance for mortal spellcasters aligned with the Nine Hells.

Slumbering (Uncommon). You have resistance to fire and necrotic damage. While you are holding the rod, it can function as a spellcasting focus for all your spells.

Stirring (Rare). The Stirring rod has the Slumbering property. In addition, Whenever you use a spell slot to cast a spell that deals fire or necrotic damage, roll a d6, and you gain a bonus equal to the roll to one of the spell's damage rolls.

Wakened (Very Rare). The Wakened rod has the Slumbering and Stirring properties, and while you are holding a Wakened focus, you can use it to cast Hellish Rebuke as a 4th level spell and Hold Monster. Once the rod is used to cast a given spell, it can’t be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn.

Ascendant (Legendary). The Ascendant rod has the Slumbering, Stirring, and Wakened properties. In addition, whenever you cast a spell that deals fire or necrotic damage, you can use the rod to deal the maximum damage instead of rolling. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.

Champion's Belt

Wondrous Item, uncommon (+1), rare (+2), or very rare (+3) (Requires Attunement by a Pugilist)

This finely detailed belt sports a large crest acknowledging the wearer as a great brawler. While wearing it, you gain a bonus to the saving throw DCs of your moxie features. The bonus is determined by the belt’s rarity. In addition, you can use an action to regain moxie points equal to a roll of your Fisticuffs die. You can’t use this action again until the next dawn.

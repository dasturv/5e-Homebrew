Potential AcceleratorVala

Wondrous Item, uncommon (Requires Attunement)

This odd machine is designed to be held in two hands. Lights of constantly shifting hues radiate from within the machine.

Flux Powered. The machine requires flux power cells to function. Each cell provides 7 charges. It takes one action to replace the cell in the machine.

Flux Orb. As an action you can expend at least one charge to cast the Chromatic Orb spell using the machine. The spell is cast at 1st level (Spell attack bonus +5), and deals a random damage type based on the table below. For each charge expended beyond the first, you can increase the spell level or attack bonus by 1.

Malfunction. Each time you attempt to cast a spell using the machine, roll a d10. If the roll is less than the number of charges the machine has expended since you last completed a long rest, the machine catastrophically malfunctions, releasing all of its energy in a bright flash of light. The spell is not cast, and instead all creatures within 20 feet of you (including you) must make a Constitution saving throw (DC 13 plus the number of charges the machine still has) or be blinded for one minute. A creature can repeat this saving throw at the start of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. After malfunctioning, the machine's energy cell is drained and the machine cannot be used again until it is repaired as part of a long rest (requires proficiency with tinker's tools).

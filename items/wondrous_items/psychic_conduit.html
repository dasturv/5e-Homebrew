Psychic Conduit

Wondrous Item, uncommon (+1), rare (+2), or very rare (+3) (Requires Attunement by a Mystic)

This finely crafted head band is intricately carved and made of silver. While wearing this head band you gain a bonus to psionic attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your psionic practices. This bonus is determined by the head band's rarity.

In addition, when you use your Mystical Recovery feature, you can increase the number of psi points you regain by 1.

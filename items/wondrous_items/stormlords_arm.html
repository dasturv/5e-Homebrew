Arm of the Storm Lord

Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement)

A finely crafted prosthetic arm made of polished wood and engraved in runes, this item replaces a lost limb a hand or arm. While the prosthetic is attached and attuned, it functions identically to the part it replaces. You can detach or reattach it as an action, and it can't be removed against your will. It detaches if you die or unattune from the item.

The arm has 15 charges, and recovers 1d10 plus 5 charges each day at dawn. While attuned to the arm, you can use an action and expend charges to cast one of the following spells: Shocking Grasp (0 charges), Gust of Wind (2 charges), Warding Wind (2 charges), Lightning Bolt (3 charges), Thunder Step (3 charges), Wind Wall (3 charges), or Storm Sphere (4 charges). Each spell has a number of charges required to cast it, and you cannot cast a spell if the arm does not have enough charges.

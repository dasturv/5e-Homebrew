Reflection Field GeneratorVala

Wondrous Item, rare (Requires Attunement)

This odd machine is designed to be worn on the back, with brass orbs protruding from it. It hums quietly.

Flux Powered. The machine requires greater flux power cells to function. Each cell provides 30 charges. It takes one complex action to replace the cell in the machine.

Reflection Field. The machine generates a field of faintly glowing energy around the wearer. This field has 30 hit points. Whenever you would take damage that isn't psychic damage, the barrier takes the damage instead. If this damage reduces the field to 0 hit points, you take any remaining damage. Every 10 minutes the machine expends one charge and regains 1 hit point, provided it has at least one charge and is missing at least one hit point.

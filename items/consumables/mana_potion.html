Potion of Mana

Potion, rarity varies

When you drink this potion, you recover expended spell slots. The spell slots can have a maximum combined level depending on this potion's rarity, as shown on the Potion of Mana table. None of the recovered spell slots can be 6th level or higher. Whatever its potency, the potion's blue liquid shimmers when agitated.

Additionally, if you do not have a spell slot of the level of this potion's minimum spell slot (as shown in the Potion of Mana table) when you drink this potion, or have drank a Potion of Mana within the last hour, you also take force damage equal to three times the number rolled. This damage cannot be reduced in any way.

Alternatively, if you are using the Spell Points variant rule, you can instead gain a number of spell points as shown on the Potion of Mana table. When you take force damage from this potion while using this variant rule, you take twice the number rolled as force damage instead of three times the number.

Hoarfrost Hammer

Weapon (maul), rarity varies (requires attunement)

This maul is cold to the touch and is covered in a thin layer of frost.

Slumbering (Uncommon). Whenever you score a critical hit on a creature with this weapon, the target's movement speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

Stirring (Rare). The Stirring hammer has the Slumbering property. In addition, you gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made using the hammer. On a hit, the hammer deals an extra 1d8 cold damage.

Wakened (Very Rare). The Wakened weapon has the Slumbering property, and it improves on the Stirring property. The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2.

As an action, you can unleash a 30-foot cone of destructive energy from the weapon. Each creature in that area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 4d6 cold damage and 4d6 thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Ascendant (Legendary). The Ascendant weapon has the Slumbering property, and it improves on the Stirring and Wakened properties. The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +3.

The cone of destructive energy the weapon creates increases to a 60-foot cone, the save DC increases to 18, and the damage increases to 6d6 cold and thunder damage.
